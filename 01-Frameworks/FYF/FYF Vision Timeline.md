# FYF Vision Timeline

Pair this with teaching on “Manifestation Proof”

Timeline
- Which topic do you have most activation around “desire” whether that desire is to escape fear or excitement. What do you want MOST in life?
- Love Relationship
- Difficult Relationship
- Parenting
- Money
- Body
- Business idea/change
- New job
- Purpose
- If it’s not one of those categories, let me know

- Mark Now on timeline
- Write underneath it
- Current Reality- Facts/descriptions
- De bt
- Declining
- Income source dwindling/ramping/steady
- Dominant Vibe about the current reality you just bulleted and how it would feel if this remained in next day, months, years  Worried, Concerned, Despair, Hopeful, Impatient, Excited
-  At the other end of the timeline what’s the endpoint that “worked out amazing” look like where the activation is now low as in it doesn’t bother you anymore or it’s High, but in a way you love and want to feel as an every day norm. 
- Write under it
- Future reality- Facts/descriptions that are observable proof what does the thing you want most look like when it moves from wanting to now having in its full completion before you can continue on and want more from that baseline? Meaning what’s the furthest out that you can go that is conceivable in having without knowing how you are going to get it? But it must be conceivable in this lifetime. The timeline and point will push out as you take action. It will feel like a moving target 
- In a new job
- Launched a program 
- Discovered my life’s work and on the path to it
- Out of debt (if exciting)
- Beach ready body
- Bloodwork normal
- Weight reached
- Reconciled relationship 
- Connected and supportive love life
- What’s the point of wanting this? Purpose rooted in values, impact, self or world. Big picture. 
- what will be the strategic byproducts (soft proof that slowly develops) of ATTEMPTING this goal? And I really like to use the word attempting the goal because I think a lot of times we get confused and we say what are the strategic byproducts of achieving the goal. And while you’re on the journey, you want to be having byproducts, you want to be having benefits, you want to be seeing some return on your investment as you’re going because if you have to wait until the very end, it’s much more challenging.
- Dominant Vibe about the Future reality you just bulleted and how it would feel if this was your normal mainstay Hopeful, Proud, Excited, Eager, In Awe, Unstoppable, Bulletproof, Joyful, Satisfied, grateful, Peaceful, Calm and centered
- Litmus: Would you want to live in this feeling every day? Would you want to feel this consistently? What thought immediately rises up almost to speak it outloud like ….bring it!  Hell yes!  Can’t stop me!  It’s happening!  Meant for me!  Built for this! Yeeeeessssss!
- Mark three evenly spaced lines between now and future
- Do chunking same for other 3 stages. What seems like a reasonable, natural progression starting with what’s your next train stop in the direction you are going?
- What’s the smallest most believable version of your desire that would already feel like relief, satisfaction, or proud? What would be fun to experience if you already trusted it was all working out? If you didn’t need to prove anything or fix anything, what would feel like a gentle next step of expression for you?
- Fill in details, byproducts of attempting and Vibe
- And then what feels on the horizon after that? Stage 2
- And then what’s the destination just before the final destination where it’s all complete? Stage 3
- You now can never say you don’t have a plan and know exactly what you are manifesting. Everyone someone asks it should not take an ounce of effort when someone asks what’s up you say I’m working on ….whatever is in Stage 1
- Between Now Reality and Stage 1 evenly space 3 lines.  Chunk it down listing 3 ideas that bridge the gap
- Do they feel doable especially the first mark because that’s now your new thing that when you talk about what you’re creating it’s that. 
- Underneath it write five things that are attached to each of those marks and underneath it confirm that it serves the goal of each of the  micro steps of Stage one.
- When you get to stage one, you will be a different identity that matches the byproducts you wrote under stage one and from that lens, you will reassess stage two to retrofit the new identity’s desire.
