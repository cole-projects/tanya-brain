# FYF BE the Source


Mini Future-Casting: From Outsourcing → Embodiment
Step 1: Scan the Areas (2 minutes)
Write these down and don’t overthink it:
- Health / Body
- Relationships
- Work / Money
- Social / Community
- Self / Inner Life
👉 Next to each one, rate how essential it feels right now on a scale of 1–10.

Step 2: “Why I Can’t Live Without This” (5 minutes)
For any area that feels like a 7–10, complete this sentence honestly:
“I feel like I can’t live without this because it gives me…”
Examples:
- Safety
- Stability
- Validation
- Identity
- Belonging
- Worth
- Motivation
- Aliveness
👉 List as many reasons as come up, even if they sound messy or uncomfortable.

Step 3: Name the Outsourced State (3 minutes)
Now underline the feelings or states, not the person or thing.
Then complete:
“The state I’m actually outsourcing here is…”
Examples:
- “Feeling safe in my body”
- “Feeling chosen”
- “Feeling financially relaxed”
- “Feeling important”
- “Feeling connected” 
“Oh… it’s not the relationship. It’s the feeling.”

Step 4: Reclaim 5% Today (3 minutes)
Finish this sentence very practically:
“One small way I can generate 5% of this feeling inside myself today is…”
Keep it simple and embodied, not aspirational.
Examples:
- Safety → slowing breathing, placing a hand on the chest
- Worth → finishing one task and letting it be enough
- Connection → sending one honest text
- Stability → organizing one small thing or checking the bank balance calmly
- Aliveness → a 5-minute walk, music, movement
You’re not trying to fix the area.
You’re teaching your body how to hold the feeling so the area can respond naturally
If the feeling comes during the action, it’s embodiment.
If it comes after the response, it’s outsourcing.
⚠️ Emphasize:
This is not about replacing the external source.
It’s about equalizing the power.

Step 5: Reframe the Relationship (1 minute)
End with this integration line:
“When I meet myself in this state, this area of my life becomes a match, not a source.”
Have them write it once for the area that stood out most.

“When you stop outsourcing a feeling and start embodying it, the external thing doesn’t disappear—it stabilizes.”Aa as as a v Casa as
You become the conduit for it to flow to. Money, love, Health flows from low to high. All of it is energy, and it is looking for a conduit that it can translate through to become more, expanded, to grow.
Money, love, Health do not create your state, it amplify what you already are
