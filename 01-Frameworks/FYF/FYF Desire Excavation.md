Phase 3: Translation 
Now you bridge fantasy → congruence.
Which of these desires are identity-based, not money-based?
What part of this future is waiting on money—and what part is waiting on the person you believe you need to be?
Identify by a circle for money and square for identity based. 
Money-based desires require external resources to exist- they are about what you have
Identity-based desires require internal permission to be expressed- they are about how you want to feel
Example:
- Traveling first class (money-based)
- Feeling taken care of, relaxed, or valued (identity-based) 
Most people unconsciously treat money as the gatekeeper of identity so those questions dismantle that illusion.
It’s helpful to spot these differences In order to:
- Return power to you rather than keeping money in the authority position
- To have a desire that feels reachable not just inspiring 
- Have your Identity move into the present
- Money becomes a tool, not a prerequisite
This is agency recovery and reclamation. 

Here are clean diagnostic questions you can use to check if something is money based vs identity based. 
Question 1:
“If money were suddenly removed again, would this disappear?”
- If yes → likely money-based
- If no → identity-based
Example:
- Hiring a full-time assistant → money-based
- Leading with more calm, creativity, or confidence → identity-based 

Question 2:
“Is this about what I’m doing… or who I’m being while I do it?”
- Doing = often money-based
- Being = almost always identity-based
Example:
- Owning a beach house → money-based
- Feeling spacious, unhurried, or playful → identity-based 

Question 3:
“Does this require permission from the world—or permission from myself?”
- World permission → money-based 
- Self permission → identity-based

This isn’t about getting you to not want the things that are money based. It’s simply an order of operations  
Money will AMPLIFY what expression of yourself you’re already living. So if you don’t want more of the life you are living then we need to first look at identity because identity CREATES how you express.
Money lets you do things at a larger scale (more, faster, bigger, outsourced).
Identity determines how you show up, what you choose, what you allow, and how you move through life — and that can be expressed right now, at any scale.
You don’t need more money to:
- live slower
- be more confident
- create honestly
- set better boundaries
- feel free in how you work
The problem arises when money is being used to create expression of what hasn’t been an embodied identity yet. 
Receiving money before an identity that can hold it, is an unsustainable and non repeatable framework that’s dependent on something outside you. Identity is an inside out attraction that becomes regenerative and self sourced.
