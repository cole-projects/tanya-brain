# FYF Meditation Alignment

Take a minute to sit with this question and answer
Alignment is…..
How do you get in alignment?
What are you aligned to when you are in alignment?
How do you know you’re in alignment?
Today we are talking about Futurecasting not an Identity of you but a more foundational you that is infused in all of the future-you versions that will continue to unfold. I’m talking about the past you, the present you and the future you that is a witness to all the versions of you in the roles you play, the levels you achieve, the various new manifested realities that you step into and become. 
First I want to clearly articulate the difference between the identity of you vs the Source-awareness of you. They are connected but not the same.
Identity is the version of you you’re choosing to become in the human game — the thoughts, behaviors, and emotional patterns that match the reality you want.
Source-awareness is the you remembering the truth underneath all identities — remembering that you are the extension of Source that is directing your mind and body.
Alignment happens when the identity you choose is grounded in the truth of your source awareness.
You are both energy(non physical being) and matter(physical human). A human being, being human. 
You can’t fully align your mind, heart, nervous system, and future identity unless they’re anchored in the deeper truth of who you actually are.
What you actually are. 
All the parts can “line up” temporarily, but without Source-awareness as the root, but the alignment isn’t stable — it wobbles, it depends on circumstances, and is easy to derail.
True alignment begins with remembering who you are.what you are. The fullness of you. 
Everything else (beliefs, nervous system, identity, action, even energy work ) organizes itself around that.
Once you remember that, every other tool works, every identity shift lands, and every alignment practice becomes natural instead of forced.
It’s the root of all practices. THE SOURCE. AND THAT SOURCE IS YOU. not something that YOU….do. The rest are just pathways back to that remembering.
It isnt about “who you are” in the personality sense.
It’s what you are in the essence sense
You are a conscious, creative, Source-extension that temporarily plays a human role. You are both form and the formless.
ALIGNMENT is the state in which your intention, integrity, and energy are congruent — even when your emotions or circumstances are stretching you.
And from that seat…everything turns to gold. It stops being resistant and becomes fuel. 
Without alignment energy work will feel like coping, fantasy, pretending. 
Alignment means that the why behind your movement is rooted in a deeper broader truth of the completeness that makes up YOU, not escape from the old or existing truth of a circumstance. 
You might still feel discomfort — but the driver of the action is your alignment; your inner compass, not your panic.
It’s when your body, mind, and spirit are moving in the same direction, even if the road feels steep.
Alignment is the prerequisite in order to coherently choose your type of action.
Alignment isn’t one side of a coin. It’s the centerpoint—the state of inner congruence from which self care, rewriting, and action become clear and effective. 
Its the intuitive dashboard — the space where you can sense whether the next step is energetic (shift how you’re being) or tangible (shift what you’re doing and take clean, aligned action).or both.  
Definition
Alignment is a receptive allowing knowing awareness of both your human and your being and the capacity to hold all available choices without self-judgment—tuning into whether the next best move is an energetic shift or a tangible action in order for your human and your being to remain whole and. Obedient, based on what allows your energy to move cleanly and clearly toward what you want.
Once you’re back in that knowing of that then rewriting becomes the tool that aligns you with:
- a new perspective,
- a new meaning,
- a new possible trajectory,
- a higher truth about the situation. 
Rewriting is about shifting the relationship to the problem and aligning with a desired direction or feeling.
Alignment reconnects you to YOU; to the whole you so that when you do the rewrite it fully lands and deepens the layers of you. It’s stretches you deeper into felt and experience of  alignment. 
Rewriting and future casting reconnects you to POSSIBILITY of an identity of you.
If you try to rewrite or futurecast before aligning with your source awareness part of you and do it only through the mind, heart and body of you it’s no different than trying to get money to feel secure or get love to feel like you belong. You’re just outsourcing your needs to something outside of you. And that won’t ever feel like power because you’re steadiness is conditional on the practice itself, and trying to align with a solution before YOURE aligned with yourself will still leave your nervous system dysregulated and the rewrite will feel fake, forced, or hollow so when we say alignment before action, it means alignment to yourself. True non judgmental connection to the non physical aspect of you, your broader perspective, the witness, the watcher, the narrator. The steady unwavering you that is always a neutral fly on the wall to your life. 
When you align first  that allows your system to easily open, your perspective widens, your inner guidance turns back on and the rewrite finally “lands” in a deeply transformative and felt way. It transmutes energy and suddenly your identity is free to become something more.  This is why rewriting “sometimes works” and “sometimes doesn’t.” 
It only works when self-connection is restored first.
This is the yin, the replenishing mode. 
The things people call alignment are expressions or byproducts of alignment, not alignment itself.
They are the effects, or byproducts, not the essence.
They’re what happens after you return to the aware you — not what alignment is.
WHAT ALIGNMENT IS NOT
Alignment is not:
- positive thinking 
- feeling good 
- emotional regulation 
- belief rewrites 
- identity upgrades 
- high vibration 
- perfect behavior 
- “doing it right” 
- a polished or grounded mindset  

✨ What Alignment Is
- remembering the You who is aware 
- resting in your Still Self / True Self 
- being the presence behind the mind 
- experiencing the quiet, steady witness 
- dropping into the broader perspective 
- awareness becoming primary, thought becoming secondary 
- being the non-physical consciousness wearing the body 
- clarity without forcing clarity 
- the internal “click” into truth 
- Being connected to your broader, non-physical perspective 
- Holding the moment without judgment of it or being in it 
- Feeling emotionally honest and energetically clear 
- Being the observer, not the story 
- Being present in the Now instead of in past/future loops 
- Allowing emotions to move, not get stuck 
- Following inner guidance over outer pressure 
- Letting your Inner Being / Higher Self lead the physical part of you 
- Feeling open, receptive, and connected (even if you’re still scared) 
- Feeling internally guided rather than externally pulled 
- Knowing “I am safe,” regardless of circumstances 
- Holding your own frequency instead of matching your environment 
- Seeing possibilities instead of threats 
Alignment = You in awareness, not you in identity.
Everything else is just the ripple that comes after.
Rewrites work on the mind.
Futurecasting works on the imagination.
But alignment happens beneath both.
It’s the space before thought enters. 
Think of it like two layers of alignment:
1. Alignment at the origin
This is the moment you remember the non-physical you —
the still, aware, resistance-free presence.
That remembering is alignment itself.
It’s the root.
The source.
The origin state.
2. Alignment in expression
Once you’re in that origin alignment, your mind, body, nervous system, and actions naturally begin to follow it.
That “whole system working together with awareness leading”
It is the outcome or expression of alignment.
🌿 THE STILL CORE MEDITATION 
Let your body settle into a comfortable position.
No need to hold yourself together.
No need to perform calmness.
Just arrive — exactly as you are.
Take a slow breath in…
and let your exhale fall out of you like a soft release.
Let your whole system know:
we are not rushing here.
Let your shoulders melt downward.
Let your jaw loosen.
Let your belly soften.
You don’t have to protect yourself in this moment.
You’re safe.”

As your breath settles, begin to notice the simple fact that you are aware.
Not aware of anything in particular…
just aware.
A soft presence behind it all.”
“Don’t try to locate it.
Let it appear on its own, like turning toward the warmth of the sun.”
“Thoughts may float through.
Emotions may rise.
Sensations may shift.
Let all of it be.
You’re not here to manage your inner world —you’re here to recognize the one who is aware of it.”

The You who is not the body.
Not the mind.
Not the emotions.
But the awareness that notices all of it.”
“As you rest in this awareness…
Let your nervous system soften.
Let Your mind stop gripping.
Feel the spaciousness
Allow Options to become visible.
Invite Clarity to arise. 
Here, Inspired action becomes obvious.
Your energy becomes coherent.
Your power returns.
Manifestation accelerates.”
“Because you’re not living as the thinker — you’re living as the one who is aware of the thinking.
Not living as the emotion — but as the one who feels it arise and pass.
Not living as the identity — but as the presence wearing it.
“Everything becomes easier when you come home to this aware You.”

“Beneath the thoughts… beneath the emotions… beneath the identity you wear…
there is a quiet center inside you.
A presence that doesn’t push or pull.
A presence untouched by fear, time, or circumstance.”
“This is your Still Core.
The essence of what you are before life happens,
before conditioning shapes you,
before the world asks anything of you.
“Feel into it now — the open, steady space behind your experience.
Not the mind.
Not the story.
The awareness that holds it all.

“Let your awareness widen…
until you feel that this steady presence has no edges.
It’s open.
Soft.
Everywhere and nowhere at once.”
“Notice how it’s not tense.
Not pressured.
Not afraid.
It simply is.”
“This is you —
the non-physical consciousness wearing a human experience.
Let yourself feel how ancient this presence is…
how unbroken…
how quietly powerful.

“From this Still Core, you don’t chase alignment.
You ARE alignment.
You don’t chase clarity.
You are clarity.
You don’t chase worth.
You are the source of worth.
Feel how every thought you think from here becomes clean.
Every choice you make from here becomes wise.
Every action you take from here becomes aligned.
“Life no longer comes at you from this place.
Life moves with you.

“Take a moment now to feel the difference between:
reacting from fear…
and responding from your Still Core. From your true essence. 
“When you respond from fear, everything feels tight, urgent, cloudy.
When you respond from your Still Core, everything feels spacious, possible, grounded.
This is the place you return to before making decisions.
Before having hard conversations.
Before taking action.
Before deciding if you need to rewrite, futurecast or self care. 
Before stepping forward into anything that matters.
The Still Core is your home base —
the place you live from, not just visit.

Take one more slow breath…
and let the Still Core expand through your entire body.
Let it fill your chest…
your belly…
your arms…
your legs…
your face…
your whole field.
And feel this truth settle into you:
‘I am not who life taught me to be.
I am what I have always been —
a steady, clear presence capable of moving through this world with power and peace.
Let that truth anchor in you.
Let it rewrite the way you show up.
Let it become the place you live from. Tour come-from that determines your outcome. 
And when you’re ready… slowly open your eyes.
Keep the Still Core with you.
It’s not something you visit.
It’s something you are.

Activity
So the fastest way back into alignment is to return to the You who is aware
Not the thought
Not the emotion
Not the story
Not the future
Not the belief
…but the awareness noticing all of it.
That shift alone recalibrates:
- your nervous system 
- your clarity 
- your energy field 
- your ability to sense next steps 
- your access to inspired action 
It’s immediate. It’s clean. It’s direct.
The fastest way to alignment is to bypass the mind entirely.
1. Drop into your Still Self
Feel the awareness behind your thoughts.
Notice the narrator in a mirror
That is alignment in its most original form.
2. Sense the space between thoughts
That gap is the stillness of alignment.
The gap before a thought is soft and open… and the gap after a thought is just as open, just as still.
Let yourself sense how both moments — the pause before and the ease after — feel spacious in the same way, while the thought itself feels like a brief movement passing through.
And as you sit here, begin to sense what a thought actually feels like.
It’s not solid… it’s more like a gentle ripple, a small flutter of energy that rises for a moment and then moves on.
You might feel it as a slight pull, a faint shift in attention, a tiny wave that rolls through and dissolves.
Let yourself notice how quickly it comes and goes… how it never stays, never holds its shape.
It’s just movement — a passing current in a much larger stillness.
There’s a silence between thoughts
— a gap that’s always there.

3. Notice the witness
“Who is aware of this thought right now?”
Who would I be without this thought?
This places you instantly in presence.

THESE ARE EFFICIENT and direct
Because rewrites + futurecasting require you to work with content:
- the story
- the belief
- the imagery
- the narrative
- the emotion 
Alignment requires no content at all. It is:
awareness
presence
stillness
clarity
non-resistance
This is why it feels effortless compared to mindset work.
“In rewrites and futurecasting, you’re shifting the content of the mind.
In alignment, you’re shifting the seat of your awareness.
You move from being inside the story
to being the awareness watching the story.
