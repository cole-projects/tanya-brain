# FYF Moments of Me2.0

A Future Casting “Moment”
Set the Frame 
Future casting isn’t about fantasy.
It’s about rehearsing who you’re becoming so your nervous system begins to recognize it as familiar instead of foreign.
This works because your brain doesn’t know the difference between vividly imagined experiences and real ones. 

It also doesn’t know the difference between what you may call a big dream vs a small dream in terms of amount of money you want to achieve, or anything contained within what you want to manifest. 

We don’t use details to “make” those details happen.  The details anchor your identity, not the outcome. Its about becoming stabilized in an identity that your brain recognizes as a safe pattern to lock in and run.

. 
So today… we’re not imagining your fully completed future.
We’re imagining a single moment with fullness and depth… because your brain accepts that more easily without argument or resistance.
The human we are identifies with measurements of less and more but in LOA the measurement stick is vibrational salience. A felt embodied reality. Not “high” vibrational as in a volume dial turned up that’s loud and hypey positive. 
Think in terms of a radio station, we are not tuning by turning up the volume more on a station we don’t like the music on or even on a static station. We are tuning to a station to deepen our experience of the music based on the quality of tone, clarity, a station or frequency that feels clear, and familiar, pleasing, enjoyable WHILE listening, not because we think listening to it will later bring us something to enjoy. We tune to station that feel more like us, like home.  
Energetic familiarity
Emotional realism to what we want to feel
Emotional congruence Normalized reality 
So when we futurecast, we are stepping into a version of us in a moment that has felt reality, not just imagination.
- How real it feels
- How natural it feels
- How believable it feels
- How settled it feels

What shifts your identity isn’t the size of the outcome — it’s the salience of the experience.
Your life changes more from a small moment that feels real…
than from a big future that feels distant.
Manifestation isn’t about how big and impressive the outcome is…
It’s about how emotionally real the feeling becomes…how meaningful, vivid, noticeable, and neurologically important the experience becomes.

So instead of imagining everything…
We’re stepping into one moment…
and rehearsing the version of you who already lives there.

In neuroscience, the word salience refers to:
- What the brain flags as important
- What stands out
- What gets prioritized and remembered
- What the brain begins organizing around

So what I’m saying is that it’s not the size of the imagined future that matters — it’s the salience of the experience WHEN you imagine it. 

Step 1 — The Path You’re On 

Imagine nothing changes.
You keep thinking the same thoughts…
Responding the same ways…
Holding the same fears…
Moving at the same pace…
Pause.
Six months from now…
You’re still living from this same emotional place.
Pause.
Now ask slowly:
• How does that version of you feel waking up in the morning?
• What are they still worrying about?
• What still feels heavy?
• What still feels unresolved?
• What feels familiar but not fully aligned?
This isn’t about judging yourself…
This is just noticing the trajectory of your current energy.
Pause.
Just let yourself feel that for a moment. In a scale 1-10 how heavy does that feel?

Step 2 — End Goal

Pick just one area right now that is on your radar:
• Career
• Finances
• Relationships
• Health
• Purpose
• Emotional well-being

If your manifestation already existed…what would be true?
Examples: State it in the present tense one line at a time. 
- I have consistent clients
- I’m confident in my offer
- People understand what I do
- I’m making steady income
- I feel relaxed about my business
List 5–10 things.

Step 3 — Reverse Engineer

For those things to be true, what had to happen first?
Examples:
- I clarified my offer
- I started talking about it
- I had conversations
- I created a landing page
- I created structure

This is where micro realities appear.

Step 4 — Identify the Closest Micro Step

Which of those feels closest…like it’s already starting…
or believable within reach?

What would you NOT be worrying about anymore?”If this were already true… What’s not running through your mind right now?

• I wouldn’t be second guessing myself
• I wouldn’t be wondering if it’s working

• I wouldn’t feel behind

Step 2 — Then Ask About Behavior

• I’d move slower

• I’d act on my decisions
• I’d stop overthinking

Step 3 — Then Ask About Self-Relationship
“How would you treat yourself differently?”
How do you talk to yourself now?”
Examples:
• I’d be kinder to myself
• I’d trust myself more
• I’d stop doubting every decision
• I’d feel more settled
