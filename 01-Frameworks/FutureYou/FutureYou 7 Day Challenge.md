## The Formula: To INDUCE a Magnetic State

### 1. Believable & Possible (not too big a stretch)

One of the most powerful ways we learn to walk our talk is by understanding how bold vision and micro steps work together.

You may have heard the idea "10x is easier than 1x." When you aim for a truly bold, exciting vision of the life you want, it actually helps you clean house. You can more clearly see what no longer fits — the old habits, projects, obligations, patterns — things you might have tried to drag along if your goal was smaller or more incremental.

A big vision forces clarity. It helps you stop wasting time on things that don't belong in the life you're becoming.

But your nervous system still needs time to normalize that change. This is where believable steps and micro actions matter. They help your body feel a growing sense of familiarity and safety with the new identity you're stepping into.

- If the vision feels like fantasy and too far away or unrealistic — the mind disconnects from the vision and won't engage, and the field won't respond. The electrical signal won't reach the field in a strong enough way to engage a magnetic pull.
- **Antidote:** Cast closer (3 months ahead) — create a believability window of scenes and moments your mind can imagine and believe being possible that you can emotionally connect with now. Chunk it.
- When it's too big or abstract, the nervous system stays in "yeah, right" mode and doesn't believe it, so it wobbles.
- The goal is to cast just far enough ahead that your mind can conceive it as possible for you.

Instead of fantasizing about "I own 5 businesses and a villa" — you future cast "I run one successful program this year." Now the mind says: "That could really happen."

**Believability** is about your MIND — can you actually picture this happening for you?
**Safety** is about your BODY — does this feel safe and receivable now, or does your system tense up, shut down, or resist it?

We want BOTH: a vision that feels possible in your mind, AND safe in your body.

---

### 2. Familiarity = Safe

**Feels familiar & safe to the nervous system.**

If it feels threatening, risky, or too foreign, your body will block or resist it — even if you consciously want it. Future casting only works when you embody what is real and AVAILABLE to you NOW.

- **Antidote:** Take micro steps — small consistent actions signal safety and help the body allow the shift.
- Choose sensory sip tastes or scenes that feel like normal life for Future You — not extreme fantasy.
- Repetition of reliving the boomerang moment helps to normalize it so that it becomes who you are, not just who you visit.

Micro actions are not about the SIZE of the vision — they are about creating bodily safety with ANY action that reflects the new identity. The field only registers the vibration, not the specific action itself.

Small steps gradually teach the body: "Oh — this is safe… I can handle this… nothing bad is happening…"

- Casting closer helps you IMAGINE something believable now.
- Micro steps help you DO something safely now.

---

### 3. Emotionally Rich & Sensory Anchored (not flat mental images)

Future casting works when your body feels the emotions and sensory experience of the future — not when it's a dry mental picture.

- **Antidote:** Engage the senses — imagine sights, sounds, feelings, textures, emotions — and let your body FEEL it as real. This creates visceral ownership — and your body starts to believe, "I know this experience."

We emotionally and sensory anchor in two ways:
- First — when we IMAGINE our future, we bring in senses + emotions so the field can feel it.
- Second — when we ACT as Future Us, we give our bodies real-life experiences that help stabilize it.

That's why we're going to be doing both — journaling and the 7-day challenge — so the identity can really land.

- If we ONLY imagine with emotion, but never act — it stays in the mind.
- If we ONLY act, but don't imagine to expand our mind — we can unconsciously recreate old patterns.
- When we do BOTH — field with our body and our mind — it locks in an emotional signature, a felt frequency of the experience.

That's true manifestation.

---

### 4. Anchored in Identity ("this is who I am or becoming")

You must genuinely start seeing yourself as this version — not just wishing or pretending — or else the field will keep reflecting the old you.

- **Antidote:** Use identity-based language (I am, I feel, I choose)… act, speak, and move as this version now.
- You're speaking as the version of you who already lives it, not talking about it.
- If you're trying to project into the future, but you're still identifying as "the version of me who doesn't have this yet," this subtly reinforces lack energy.

Example: "When I have more money I'll feel free and calm." This places calm/freedom out there, which keeps it out of reach vibrationally. This is outsourcing your emotion to the thing or making your desired emotion conditional on the thing.

Instead: speak and feel from the version of you who already is that person.

Think of it like an actor rehearsing for a role. You don't wait until opening night to become the character — you rehearse the scenes, the emotions, the movements, until your body knows them. That's what future casting does: you're rehearsing the emotional state, the identity, the felt experience of your desired future — so your mind, body, and field begin to live it now.

---

### 5. Repeated & Normalized (enough exposure for your system to stabilize it)

Repetition turns "this is exciting" into "this is normal" — which is what allows the new identity to stick and the field to match it.

- **Antidote:** Repeat daily — revisit feelings, identity statements, and small actions — until it starts feeling natural.

## The 3 Personal Energetics Ingredients

**Why These Keep You Showing Up:**

**1. Big WHY (emotional fuel — why it matters)**
Values, expansive reason you desire it. It's not just about having more money or more clients or more freedom — it's about being able to move through life as the version of us that's aligned, empowered, and free — who ripples that energy into the world in your unique chosen vocation.

**2. Trust in the Process (so you don't "push" or collapse into doubt)**
Trusting the process doesn't mean sitting around hoping or doing nothing. It means understanding that when you are building a new identity, results often lag behind actions. Your field and your nervous system need time to catch up and stabilize the new frequency. If you start doubting, forcing, or trying to 'check on' the outcome too soon, it scrambles the signal.

Trust simply means:
- I keep showing up.
- I let the field do its work while I do mine.
- I understand that what I'm becoming will start to reflect in my life — but the timing won't always match my calendar.

**3. Your Relationship with Contrast**
As the birthplace of your next expansion, for clarity and direction, and as a platform or mental gym — the thing that, when you possess it, makes your desire possible because it allows you to pulse a clean signal.

## The Core Pivot

Your Future You isn't created in one big leap — it's created by stabilizing the next true step that's ready to emerge. The question isn't "How do I become my entire Future Self?" — the real question is:

**What version of me is ready to emerge THIS season? What am I ready to live, breathe, and stabilize next?**

---

## Journal Prompts

**1. What is the larger vision I am moving toward — in ONE area of life?**
Choose ONE area you want to focus on for this Challenge (business, money, career, relationship, health, etc.). What is your full dream — your Future You life 1–3 years from now? Bullet point out the details.

---

**2. Why do I care about this at the deepest level — what will it allow me to express, create, or ripple into the world?**
What's my soul-driven WHY — the one that will carry me through contrast? Why will you stay committed — even on hard days?

---

**3. Does everything in my vision align with my deeper WHY?**
For each part of what you wrote — income, number of clients, what you want to create or sell — can you see a clear connection to your WHY? Does each part of the vision serve a meaningful purpose?

---

**3b. What is the believable milestone I'm stabilizing this season?**
Most of us get stuck because we try to cast way too far ahead. Break it down and name your next milestone in your big vision — this is the result or target you are stabilizing through this 7-Day Challenge. It helps you stay anchored in what you are creating NOW.

---

**4. What version of me is ready to emerge — that I haven't fully owned yet?**
This is about who you are becoming — the qualities, traits, and ways of being that match this milestone. How would this version of you move through life? What energy would you embody? Think essence — not a to-do list.

Example: "Confident… grounded… bold in sharing my voice… trusting my wisdom…"

This is about IDENTITY — essence, self-concept, the inner version of you that is ready to come forward.

---

**5. What will success LOOK and FEEL like when I reach this milestone?**
Describe the evidence of the experience — both external and internal — so your system can start normalizing it. What would you shout?

---

**6. What actions could reflect or express this version of me this week? This is your 7-day challenge.**
List 3–5 ways you could act, move, or show up each day this week that would reflect this milestone version of you. They can be small or bold, playful or serious.

Example: Post a client invitation… Write a page of the book… Introduce yourself as the title you wish to become… Wear an outfit that feels like Future You.

---

**7. What blocks, resistance, or challenges might I encounter during this 7-Day Challenge?**
What thoughts, emotions, or circumstances might get in your way? Name them now so they don't catch you off guard.

---

**8. What will I do or say to myself when these blocks show up — so I can keep moving?**
Pre-decide how you'll respond — so resistance won't stop you.

Example: "I will remind myself 5% more is a win… I'll ask, 'What would Future Me do?'… I'll take one small action even if I don't feel ready."

---

## Closing

Write "I am now a person who…" statements at the end of each day.

**Post / pulse energy — without waiting for response.**

"Post like you're reporting in to your agent or a board — not fishing for likes."

You're not posting to get validation — you're documenting your embodiment. You're showing the field, 'This is who I am becoming.' Post as if the shift is already happening — not hoping for approval.
