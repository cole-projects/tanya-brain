# FutureYou Frequency meditation on space between thoughts

## The Still Core Meditation (With the Integrated Origin-Alignment Teaching)

"Let your body settle into a comfortable position.
No need to hold yourself together.
No need to perform calmness.
Just arrive — exactly as you are."

"Take a slow breath in…
and let your exhale fall out of you like a soft release.
Let your whole system know:
we are not rushing here."

"Let your shoulders melt downward.
Let your jaw loosen.
Let your belly soften.
You don't have to protect yourself in this moment.
You're safe."

---

## Guiding Them Into Being

"As your breath settles, begin to notice the simple fact that you are aware.
Not aware of anything in particular…
just aware.
A soft presence behind it all."

"Don't try to locate it.
Let it appear on its own, like turning toward the warmth of the sun."

"Thoughts may float through.
Emotions may rise.
Sensations may shift.
Let all of it be.
You're not here to manage your inner world —
you're here to recognize the one who is aware of it."

---

## Inserting the Origin-Alignment Truth Cleanly

"And before we go deeper, let this truth wash through you:
Alignment is not positive thinking.
It isn't feeling good, or high-vibe, or perfectly regulated.
Those are expressions of alignment — not alignment itself."

"Alignment, at the deepest level, is remembering the You who is aware."

"The You who is not the body.
Not the mind.
Not the emotions.
But the awareness that notices all of it."

"When you rest in this awareness…
your nervous system softens.
Your mind stops gripping.
You feel spacious.
Options become visible.
Clarity arises.
Inspired action becomes obvious.
Your energy becomes coherent.
Your power returns.
Manifestation accelerates."

"Because you're not living as the thinker — you're living as the one who is aware of the thinking.
Not living as the emotion — but as the one who feels it arise and pass.
Not living as the identity — but as the presence wearing it."

"Everything becomes easier when you come home to this aware You."

---

## Introducing the Still Core

"Beneath the thoughts… beneath the emotions… beneath the identity you wear…
there is a quiet center inside you.
A presence that doesn't push or pull.
A presence untouched by fear, time, or circumstance."

"This is your Still Core.
The essence of what you are before life happens,
before conditioning shapes you,
before the world asks anything of you."

"Feel into it now — the open, steady space behind your experience.
Not the mind.
Not the story.
The awareness that holds it all."

---

## Letting Them Feel Their Non-Physical Consciousness

"Let your awareness widen…
until you feel that this steady presence has no edges.
It's open.
Soft.
Everywhere and nowhere at once."

"Notice how it's not tense.
Not pressured.
Not afraid.
It simply is."

"This is you —
the non-physical consciousness wearing a human experience."

"Let yourself feel how ancient this presence is…
how unbroken…
how quietly powerful."

---

## Anchoring the Manifesto-Level Truth

"From this Still Core, you don't chase alignment.
You are alignment.
You don't chase clarity.
You are clarity.
You don't chase worth.
You are the source of worth."

"Feel how every thought you think from here becomes clean.
Every choice you make from here becomes wise.
Every action you take from here becomes aligned."

"Life no longer comes at you from this place.
Life moves with you."

---

## Making It a Living Practice — Not a Temporary Meditation

"Take a moment now to feel the difference between:
reacting from fear…
and responding from the Still Core."

"When you respond from fear, everything feels tight, urgent, cloudy.
When you respond from the Still Core, everything feels spacious, possible, grounded."

"This is the place you return to before making decisions.
Before having hard conversations.
Before taking action.
Before stepping forward into anything that matters."

"The Still Core is your home base —
the place you live from, not just visit."

---

## Closing With Embodiment

"Take one more slow breath…
and let the Still Core expand through your entire body.
Let it fill your chest…
your belly…
your arms…
your legs…
your face…
your whole field."

"And feel this truth settle into you:
'I am not who life taught me to be.
I am what I have always been —
a steady, clear presence capable of moving through this world with power and peace.'"

"Let that truth anchor in you.
Let it rewrite the way you show up.
Let it become the place you live from."

"And when you're ready… slowly open your eyes.
Keep the Still Core with you.
It's not something you visit.
It's something you are."
