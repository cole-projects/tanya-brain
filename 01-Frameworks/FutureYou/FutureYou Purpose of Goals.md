# FutureYou Purpose of Goals

There's a paradox inside the Law of Attraction work. If the real "goal" is to feel the emotions now—joy, safety, confidence—then why even bother desiring the manifestation of physical things like money, health, or relationships?
1  Having goals are not about the thing. The physical goals aren't the endgame; they are permission slips that help you focus your energy.  Think of them as the translation app you pass through in order to speak "universe vibrational language" to submit your request. 
They give your mind a direction and your heart something to open toward. But the essence of what you're really after is always the state—the feeling of security, love, vitality, freedom.
2  The state comes first—but that doesn't make goals irrelevant
When you learn to feel the essence now, you're no longer hostage to circumstances. You don't need money to feel abundant, or a partner to feel loved. That alignment draws in the physical forms that resonate with your state. The goal isn't necessary for the feeling, but it's a natural outflow of the vibration you're holding. Provide a landing strip for energy to channel through in physical reality.
3  Goals let you embody spirit in matter; Energy made visible
Everything happens twice- it starts as energy — a vibration, an idea, a frequency. Spirit is formless potential; it's the raw essence of creation before it takes shape. Matter is that same energy made visible — thoughts solidified into 3D form.
When you set a goal and then align your energy with it, you're literally turning invisible vibration into physical experience.
So a goal is an energetic bridge — it's how Spirit (pure potential) moves through you to become form (a tangible result).
Without goals, your energy would just swirl in the realm of potential — beautiful, but unexpressed. Goals give your creative energy a channel.
Even if you can feel love and joy without them, when the relationship or money arrives, it expands your capacity to hold and express those states. You get to live them in technicolor, share them with others, and play with them in form. 
Without goals, life would stay in the abstract. 

Goals say to the universe:
"I'm ready to let this vibration express itself through me — not just as a feeling, but as an experience."
That's how love becomes a relationship, abundance becomes money or opportunity, inspiration becomes art or impact.
4  Embodying energy in matter is living your alignment; true connection
When you act, create, or build something from alignment — when your vibration leads — you become the instrument through which LIFE experiences itself as physical reality. Substitute life with Source energy, God, Universe, consciousness. 
So, to embody source energy in matter means you're not escaping the human world to feel spiritual — you're bringing the spiritual into the human, letting your inner energy sculpt your outer world. Human and Being. Both forms. So we're essentially living the human-scale version of E = mc².
Two sides of the same coin where matter and energy are the same essence, expressed in different forms.
Goals are how consciousness plays.  They're the playground where infinite potential becomes personal expression.
5  Desire is the engine of expansion
Through contrast, new desires/goals are constantly being born. Contrast/dips are not flaws or things gone wrong; they're the mechanism of creation itself. To stop wanting would be to stop expanding. Feeling good now isn't about ending desire—it's about enjoying the unfolding while trusting that desire is the fuel of your soul's growth.
So the point of goals isn't to fix you or to finally deliver the feelings you lack. It's to give you new canvases to experience and expand the states you've already cultivated. To expand and the enjoyment of it. 


✨ Creating the Landing Strip: Letting Energy/Spirit Take Form
To help you form goals that aren't just mental checklists or fixes, but are energetic invitations of expansion — grounded pathways for energy to flow into form.  So this exercise bridges vibration → intention → embodiment.
Step 1: Name the Frequency
Before you decide what you want, tune into how you want to feel.
Close your eyes and breathe slowly.
Ask yourself:
- What frequency do I want to live in more often?   (safety, freedom, creative flow, love, ease, certainty, play.) 
- When I imagine feeling that fully, what sensations show up in my body?   (Warmth, stillness, aliveness, openness, etc.) 
Write down your chosen Core Frequency(state) — and the vibration(felt experience) you want to embody.Choose the vibration your soul is craving to live in.
🜂 Example: 

- "Freedom — a spacious, open feeling in my chest that feels like wind and possibility."
- Ease – Feeling like everything flows smoothly, time expands, and things work out without force.
- Abundance – A deep sense of "there's more than enough," in money, love, and opportunity.
- Grounded Safety – Being at peace in your body, knowing you can trust life and yourself. 
- Radiant Confidence – Feeling lit from within, walking into rooms as your whole self. 
- Creative Flow – Ideas, expression, and inspiration pouring through you effortlessly. 
Step 2: Give It a Form
Now, imagine that frequency wanted to express itself in the physical world — let's create the emotional blueprint to give your frequency a sensory identity.
You're not living it yet; you're discovering what your chosen frequency looks and feels like when it flows through life.
This step is about being playful, intuitive, and imaginative — like a "preview reel" of the frequency. Use visual, emotional, sensory examples so your body recognizes the feeling you're calling in.
You're not deciding yet how that frequency will manifest particularly for you — you're just mapping its feeling equivalents.
Look for scenes, images, and experiences in life that match that frequency — anything that feels like the Step 1 energy.You're painting the feeling into images so your body can recognize its signature when it shows up.
You're building an emotional dictionary for that vibration. Priming.
Ask:
- If this energy could take shape, what would it look like in life, not specifically the way you desire to call it in yet but just matching equivalents.  
- What experiences or creations would naturally emerge from this frequency? 
Let the images come — don't force logic.
They might be scenes, symbols, or intuitive flashes: a home by the ocean, music, laughter, community.
You're envisioning how the frequency wants to move through life — not defining logistics yet, just translating vibration into imagery and possibility.
✅ Example:
- Freedom looks like ocean air, open time, light luggage, working from a cafe, driving with windows down, hair in the wind. Unscheduled mornings with coffee and sunlight. A spontaneous trip with no itinerary. 
- Confidence looks like bright colors, laughter, eye contact, bold choices, walking with intention.Speaking on podcasts, sharing personal stories online, being visible.Dancing in your living room   
- Safety looks like candlelight, savings cushion, soft blankets, exhale, a hug, a campfire, a reliable car, A cozy home that feels like sanctuary, steady monthly income. 
- Ease looks like a lighter schedule, afternoons off, and an assistant who handles logistics, saying no without needing to explain, calls answering the first ring 
- Abundance → Consistent high-value clients, spontaneous travel, generous tipping, filet mignon, big family gatherings, tall glasses, a full pantry, a full gas tank
- Clarity— Wide open sky and sunlight, Booking a one-way ticket  
General things of what this energy frequency could look like— the imagery that helps your body recognize what that energy feels like.

Step 3: Anchor It as a Goal (Your Landing Strip)
Now translate that energetic expression into a grounded, specific landing strip — a physical form that the universe/ quantum field can easily find and provide a match to. We move from energetic imagery to physical specificity.
Define a tangible way for that energy to take shape — the measurable expression.
This step lets your nervous system and imagination taste what that specific expression of energy feels like before it lands. Normalizing it in your body to increase your permission and capacity to hold its fullness when it arrives. The dress rehearsal. Must feel good
Ask:
- What's a tangible way I can invite this energy to land? 
- What would it look like for me to receive this energy through something in 3D reality?
You're not chasing the goal — you're creating a space, an invitation of a physical 3D structure, where the vibration can crystallize into form. An open channel 
This step is about defining the form that your energy can land in that also allows your nervous system to stay on board with your frequency. Intention plus elevated emotion.
You're saying:
"Here's how this energy can express itself through me in tangible reality."
It's the vision statement or specific outcome — the coaching business, the home, the partnership, the book, the income level, the creative project.
It's your way of declaring to the Universe:
"I'm open to this vibration taking this kind of form."
Think: "What will it look like when this energy touches down?"
This is where you choose a form that Source energy can actually use — a concrete, measurable invitation point in 3D reality.
You're saying:
"Here's how that energy could land in matter — through this goal, project, or creation."
✅ Example:
- "I'm creating a landing strip for Freedom by running a coaching business that only requires 5 hours per day and the rest of the time to be creative. 
- "I'm creating a landing strip for Safety by paying off $10K debt and building a 3-month buffer."
- "I'm creating a landing strip for Confidence by booking one speaking gig this quarter. Or being visible on social media"
- I'm creating a landing strip for Ease by working 25 hours a week and outsourcing admin."
- I'm creating a landing strip for Abundance by welcoming 5 aligned clients at $3K each."
- "I'm creating a landing strip for Radiant Confidence by launching my first live workshop."
- "I'm creating a landing strip for Creative Flow by committing to a daily 30-minute writing ritual." 
It's clear, measurable, and grounded enough to give energy somewhere to flow and meet you.
It's the bridge point between energy and matter. 

Step 4: Align the Atmosphere
Once you've built the "runway," this step is about matching the frequency that allows the plane to land. Integration. 
You're now curating the inner and outer environment — the emotions, thoughts, and micro-actions that harmonize with the vibration you called in.
It's where you begin living the energy now so that energy recognizes the match and flows naturally into it.
Think: "How do I become the field that makes this goal inevitable?"
Write 2–3 practices or micro-actions that help you hold that vibration now.
Ask yourself:
- What would it look like to already be living this? 
- What daily emotions or actions would match that energy? 
🜂 Example:
- Ease – Slow morning coffee, no-scroll mornings, soft background music while you work. 
- Abundance – Daily gratitude for what's already here, tipping extra. 
- Safety – Evening walks, journaling "I am safe to receive," staying off anxiety loops. 
- Confidence – Post an unfiltered photo, practice "heart-open posture," speak up once daily.
- Morning breathwork 
- Working from a café twice a week to feel mobility now.
- Saying no to obligations that don't align with freedom.
- Creating spaciousness in your calendar
- Moving your body outdoors
- Decluttering your calendar to create open space
- Taking your laptop to a café to work from anywhere
- Saying "no" to one draining obligation
- Driving with the windows down just because it feels free
- Spending an hour on a spontaneous creative project 
It's not envisioning what the frequency could feel like; it's embodying it in real time.
You're asking: "What can I physically do — today — that represents this frequency in action?"
This is where the vibration meets daily life.
These are the micro-actions, habits, and energetic choices that make your goal inevitable because they match its frequency. The embodiment phase — living the vibration and walking the talk.
Act, think, and feel as the frequency now.

Step 5: Seal with a Declaration
End with this affirmation:
I am the meeting point of energy and matter.
My goals are sacred containers for energy to express through me.
I don't chase what I want — I become the space where it lands.
Take one deep breath and imagine light descending into your heart — Spirit touching down.
Claim your role as the bridge between energy and form.
- "I am the calm channel where Ease flows into everything I touch."
- "I am abundance in motion — life multiplies through my openness."
- "I am grounded in divine safety; the universe builds upon my steadiness."
- "I am the living proof that confidence is light embodied."
- "I am the vessel through which creative energy becomes tangible art."
