# FutureYou Dealers Choice V1

## The Science Behind Futurecasting

Before we move into the embodiment part of today's call, I want to help you really understand what's happening inside you when you try to build the energy of the life you want, because it's helpful to understand how our nervous system actually works. When we do, then we are better able to see that this process isn't personal — it's mechanical.

If it feels hard, it's not because you're broken — it's because your brain is wired to favor survival, not expansion. We're just retraining the wiring. Your brain isn't broken — it's doing exactly what it was designed to do: keep you safe. But thriving takes different wiring. Futurecasting teaches your brain and body that it's safe to expand, not just survive. So we're giving your brain a new job description: A Thriver.

---

### 1. You Have a Negativity Bias Baked In

Your brain is wired with this negativity bias. It prioritizes scanning for threat over scanning for joy — because that's what keeps us alive. Its job is to look for what's wrong, what's missing, potential threats, and how to avoid discomfort.

Even when you manage to feel abundance, love, or joy for a moment — the survival brain will naturally pull you back very fast to fear, lack, or self-protection because unfamiliar states are treated as risky by the nervous system, EVEN if they are desirable. Unsafe trumps desire every time.

So your brain will go: "This is nice, but how long can we keep this up? Is this safe?" Or "but what if…", and before you know it, you're rehearsing old fear patterns again.

This is why it often feels like negative emotions stick more easily, and positive ones feel fleeting — it's not that positive states are weaker. It's that they are not automatic like the negativity bias so they require more conscious training to BECOME familiar and safe.

---

### 2. Mirror Neurons — Making This Worse or Better

These are special neurons that help you FEEL what you OBSERVE in others, without even experiencing it yourself. They help simulate the feeling in your body. They're why we flinch when someone gets hurt, or why we cry during movies.

Mirror neurons fire automatically, unconsciously — you don't CHOOSE to activate them.

They're why we can so easily attune to other people's negativity, fear, or lack — especially if our own system is already leaning toward survival. This is why the energy you surround yourself with matters. It's why doomscrolling matters. Watching news matters. The company you keep matters.

**The good news:** mirror neurons also allow you to borrow positive resonance from other people's wins, other's example of good, what you watch and hear that uplifts and inspires — and from visualization.

---

### The Mirror Neuron + Quantum Field Connection

The quantum field works like a giant mirror neuron system. It doesn't respond to your words — it mirrors back what you are embodying energetically.

- Mirror neurons cause your body to experience internally what you observe externally.
- The quantum field causes your external reality to reflect and reinforce what you are pulsing internally.

The field is like an externalized mirror neuron system: When you embody fear, it mirrors fear-based experiences. When you embody trust and thriving, it mirrors trust-based and thriving experiences.

**This is why Future Casting works:**
When you imagine and feel your future self and anchor it somatically, your own mirror neurons start firing as if this is who you are — reinforcing that identity internally. At the same time, you are pulsing this embodied state outward — and the quantum field begins to mirror it back through life experience.

---

## The 5 Ingredients for Futurecasting to Stick

1. **Emotional salience** — it must feel personally meaningful and alive, not flat.
2. **Repetition** — practice often, so the new state feels normal.
3. **Somatic anchoring** — feel it in your body, not just your mind.
4. **Identity-based** — embody it as who you are becoming, not just what you hope happens.
5. **Familiarity** — your system must begin treating this state as safe and known.

---

## Key Caveats

Even with this formula, there are additional layers:

1. **Universal timing** — the field mirrors back when all pieces align, not on your schedule. Radical trust is required.
2. **Congruent action** — your behaviors must match your desired state, or you send mixed signals.
3. **Clearing subconscious resistance** — unresolved blocks can pulse incoherence underneath.

**Bottom line:** Future casting is not about forcing a vision. It's about giving your system small, repeated, embodied tastes of your desired state — until it becomes familiar, safe, and normal. That's when life begins to reflect it back.

## Rockets of Desire (Abraham Hicks)

Abraham Hicks uses the phrase "rockets of desire" to describe the energetic signals we launch — usually unconsciously — when we experience contrast and become clearer about what we do want.

- When you experience something you don't want (contrast), you automatically — vibrationally — launch a corresponding rocket of desire for what you do want.
- You don't even have to verbalize it — the rocket is launched through your experience and vibration.
- These rockets go immediately into the vibrational reality (the "Vortex"). The Universe begins responding to them instantly, lining up cooperative components.

The art of manifesting is not about trying to "launch rockets" on purpose — you already do that naturally. The real work is about:
- Becoming a vibrational match to what you've already launched
- Releasing resistance that contradicts your desires
- Aligning with the version of you who already matches what you've asked for

In simple terms:
1. Contrast happens → You launch a rocket.
2. The Universe holds the vibrational reality of that desire.
3. Your work is to tune your vibration toward the desire, not against it.

## Future Casting Facilitator Version 2.0

*No meditation — fully guided journaling + sensory embodiment flow.*

**Opening Teaching:**
"Future casting is the practice of training your nervous system and energy field to feel the reality you want as if it's already here — so your body treats it as safe, familiar, and natural. And when that happens, your external reality begins to match it.

It works because your energy field responds to what you FEEL, not just what you think. The universe mirrors your dominant vibration — not your words, not your wishes, but your embodied state.

So today, we are going to help your system practice tasting that future — through guided prompts that move you from thought → to sensory detail → to embodied emotional experience.

You can't do this wrong. You don't need a perfect vision. All you need is to feel a little more of the energy you want to live in — and that is enough to begin shifting your field."

---

### Step 1: Set Your Coordinates — Define Goal

"Set your coordinates — just like a rocket needs a destination, your system needs a clear target to align with."

**Prompt:** "What is the #1 goal you want to manifest?" → 1–2 min journaling.

*Encouragement: "There's no wrong answer here. Choose what feels most alive for you today — you can always evolve it later."*

---

### Step 2: Fuel Up — Use Contrast

"Fuel up — contrast gives us clarity. We're going to use contrast not to dwell on the past, but to sharpen what we're moving toward."

**Prompt:** "What experiences or situations have caused you discomfort or dissatisfaction that led you to this desire? What would the opposite look like?" → 3–4 min journaling.

*Encouragement: "Notice how your contrast gives you clarity — this is fuel for building the right frequency."*

---

### Step 3: Ignite the Engines — Clarify Personal Mission

"Ignite the engines — your WHY is your ignition. The reason you want this gives your desire emotional power that can propel it forward."

*Teaching Frame:* "As we move into this next part, it's natural for the brain to want to answer from what you want to get rid of — but we're going to stretch beyond that. I want you to answer these next questions from the perspective of what you are creating, expanding into, and embodying — not what you're trying to fix or escape. Think growth, not rescue. Think forward, not backward."

For example — instead of 'I want more money so I'm not stressed,' think 'I want more money so I can feel freedom, joy, and generosity.' Instead of 'I want love so I'm not lonely,' think 'I want love so I can feel cherished, radiant, and co-creative.'

**Prompt:** "Why do you want this manifestation? What feelings or experiences will it create? How does this goal align with your core values or expand your life?" → 3–4 min journaling.

*Encouragement: "The more connected you are to WHY you want this, the more emotional power your vision will hold."*

---

### Step 4: Lift Off + Overcoming Inertia

"This is where we begin building the felt experience brick by brick. We'll start by anchoring into one vivid, juicy moment — a detail or action that really lights you up — and then allow that energy to expand into a fuller sensory scene. You don't need to visualize your entire life perfectly — just allow the energy of this future self to come alive in your body one moment at a time."

**Part 1 — Short written anchor (1–2 min journaling):**
"Choose one juicy detail or moment that feels exciting — one brick to build from. It could be a scene, an interaction, a way you feel, something you're doing. Write one sentence or phrase to anchor it."

*Encouragement: "You are not writing to get it perfect — you are writing to choose an entry point. One moment, one detail is enough to begin."*

**Part 2 — Transition to feeling (pens down):**
"Now put your pen down — from this point forward, this is about FEELING. I'm going to guide you to expand this energy and immerse in the sensory experience. You are building the feeling of your future self now — your only job is to feel it fully."

**Part 3 — Guided Sensory + Emotional Expansion:**

SEE: "Look around this future scene. What colors stand out? What is your environment like? You're looking at your phone or laptop — what's the notification, email, or message you're seeing?"

HEAR: "What sounds are present? Are there voices? Music? What do you hear that makes this moment feel alive? What sound do you hear — the ding of a payment notification? The voice of a happy client?"

SMELL: "Is there a scent in the air? Maybe fresh flowers, a favorite perfume, or something cooking? Maybe you're sipping coffee as you read the message — how does it smell?"

TASTE: "Are you tasting anything — a drink, a meal — that anchors you in this scene? Maybe you're tasting that first sip of coffee after a great client call."

---

### Step 5: Breaking Through the Atmosphere

"This is where you fully embody the new frequency, deeply in your body. This is where the shift happens."

**Prompts:**
- "If this is your new life — what emotions would you FEEL daily?"
- "What does this new normal feel like?"
- "What physical sensations arise in your body as you imagine this being true?"
- Does it feel like grounded confidence, relaxed shoulders, warmth, openness, tingling, expansion, lightness in your chest?
- "What emotion is rising — calm pride, ease, steady joy?"

"Let yourself linger here. The longer you can feel this, the more your nervous system learns it is safe and familiar. You are building this frequency brick by brick — this is how it becomes your new normal. If stress was gone — what are you free to feel now?"

---

### Step 6: Entering Orbit

"Here we anchor this frequency into ordinary moments of life. This is how you make it a lived reality, not just a vision."

**Prompt:** "Imagine you are holding your coffee, slipping into bed, opening the door, or doing some small, familiar gesture — in this future life scene. How does it FEEL to do this ordinary thing as the version of you living this next level?"

You could be:
- Drinking coffee
- Opening email
- Taking a shower
- Walking the dog
- Answering the phone
- Getting dressed

BUT — this time, you are doing it as the version of yourself who IS successful, who IS in that new frequency. This is your new identity who's doing it!

"This is how we anchor the extraordinary into the ordinary — this is where true embodiment happens."

---

### Step 7: Let Go of the Boosters

**Prompts:**
- "What does this mean about who you are now?"
- "What do you know to be true about yourself that you didn't before?"
- "If your heart could speak right now about this FutureYou, what would it say?"
- "If this was always meant for you, how does it feel to finally claim it?"
- "Now that you are living this reality — what else do you see as possible?"

Example responses:
- "It means I was capable of this all along — I just had to let myself become it."
- "It means that anything truly is possible when I live in alignment."
- "It means I am worthy of receiving success, ease, and abundance — not just earning it through struggle."
- "It means that I can trust myself and the universe — I don't have to control every outcome to create a beautiful life."
- "It means that this version of me was always in me — I'm not becoming someone new, I'm remembering who I am."

"Now that you've embodied this frequency, you can release old patterns that no longer serve you, and release attachment to how this will come to be.

You are not responsible for the 'how.' Your job is to embody the energy — to become the signal — and to trust divine timing and the path of least resistance where you prioritize feeling good and clean energy before action. Trust the new signal, don't decode the old noise.

As you hold this frequency, life will organize itself to match it in ways beyond what your mind can predict."

"You are not visualizing anymore — you are becoming. The more you embody this now, the more life responds — in its perfect time and way."

The more familiar it becomes, the more the universe mirrors it back — not through force, but through resonance. You are becoming the signal today."
