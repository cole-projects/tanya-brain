# FutureYou on Self Worth Presentation

Community Call: Future Casting Self-Worth & Deservedness
Total time: 30 minutes

1. Theme of the Week: Self-Worth & Deservedness (2 min)
“Today we’re future-casting the frequency of self-worth and deservedness — the feeling that you are inherently worthy, not because of the roles you play, the gifts you’ve been given, the functions you perform, but because your worthiness is your birthright. We’re going to meet the version of you who already walks in this truth—who already lives from unshakable self-worth—the part of you that knows you are deserving, not because of what you do, but because of who you are.”
“We’re future casting today — but really, we’re just remembering what’s already been true all along.”
Self-worth is fundamental to manifestation.
When you don’t feel inherently worthy or deserving,
you either block receiving (even when opportunities come),
or manifest from lack (creating results that reflect your hidden doubts and insecurities).
Whereas when you remember your intrinsic worth,
you manifest from a frequency of belonging, trust, and already-being-enough,
and the energy you send out is clear, open, magnetic.
Self-worth is not just a “nice to have” — it’s the energetic foundation for drawing in anything aligned with your desires.
Self-worth isn’t just one piece of the puzzle.
It’s actually the foundation underneath everything you could ever want to create or receive.
The more you remember that you’re already worthy — already deserving — just because you exist,
the more everything else you’re wanting can flow naturally toward you.
So today, we’re not just future-casting a vision —
we’re future-casting the energy that makes the vision possible.”
Living energetic transmission between your now-self and your true becoming where the physical version of you catches up to what the larger part of you already knows about who you are and lives FROM that energy in a future version of yourself. 
Fight from the W
We will do a short meditation that accesses a Threshold Moment where you witness your Future Self already embodied, already experiencing self worth. 
You're basically creating a quantum meeting point between who you’ve been and who you’re becoming.
2. Open with Gratitude (3 min)
“Let’s open with a quick gratitude pulse. Gratitude immediately lifts our frequency and preps us to receive more. So, let’s LET ourselves off the hook for a moment of what everyone else needed us to be and claim what we already are through gratitude for ourselves. So reach inward — toward who you ARE beyond what you have, what you’ve earned, learned, developed even. 
What’s something ABOUT you, about who you ARE naturally without trying, that maybe even someone else has appreciated… that deep down, YOU know too is true? You recognize it in tourself when they say it. 
It’s what’s natural or intrinsic within you. If your brain is inclined to jump to what you’ve earned or worked hard for, I want you to ask yourself what about the larger broader energetic YOU that you are, enabled you to earn it or work for it. What QUALITY about you allowed for you to do or have something. These are qualities that even if what you’ve learned, earned, have, gained would GO AWAY, this quality remains with you. It’s ALWAYS been there but maybe weren’t always aware of it. It’s a birthright gift endowed to you as an alignment starter kit. 
HINT: This often can be something that you shared naturally as a child and was shamed or punished for, so your brain adapted and developed coping mechanisms to resist this part of you, hide it.
