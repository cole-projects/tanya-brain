# FutureYou Q&A after 7 Day Challenge

FutureYou Q&A after 7 Day Challenge

Welcome back, everyone. This call is a little different—we’re dropping more into an open Q&A today. No heavy agenda, and moreso just a space to land, reflect, ask questions, and calibrate together.
Last week, we got really clear on what it takes for a future cast to land—not just as a vision, but as an energetic identity you walk with.
We talked about how casting a future isn’t just about imagination—it’s about practicing congruence.
Doing the thing before you have the result.
Acting from the energy of already being that version of you, even if your current circumstances haven’t caught up yet.
Then we looked at crafting  7-day FutureYou mini-challenges—not to pressure ourselves, but to give our bodies and brains a taste of who we’re becoming designed to help you walk it like your FutureYou talks it. 
It wasn’t about perfection. It was about pulsing new energy into the field—clean, simple, clear, and actionable felt lived moments of BEING AND DOING that are familiar and safe so that you ARE the version of you that already knows- that intersects your now and your future and they begin to morph and merge and transform you. 
So today is a chance to check in on what landed, tune into what’s working, what’s wobbling, and what’s trying to emerge next. This is also your chance to ask anything about future casting, resistance, energy alignment, or decision-making. 
Wherever you are, just be there with what is coming up and land there fully. 
———
One thing that I want to remind you from last week's call and you doing the challenge(to whatever degree you did it), is that you don’t need a massive RESULT to know you’re shifting and reaching your manifestation. You don’t need a massive vision, massive future casting details and you don’t need massive results. The language of the universe does not hear big or small dreams as we see it. It hears emotional charge, authenticity and consistency. So its Tiny congruent EMOTIONALLY FELT experiences or clear UNDENIABLY LIVED actions that are the proof of your manifestation coming…they are not just a signal to the universe that it has clear indicators to reflect back. So  before the manifestation even happens, the capstone for it to “hear” is YOUR sincerity to yourself, acknowledging the proof that is the accelerator to more of your manifestation revealing itself to you. (When YOU reveal yourself to IT)
Manifestation doesn’t require a giant leap. It requires tiny, lived congruence.
Every small action you took this week—and every felt emotional moment that matched your FutureYou—WAS the massive work.
That’s how timelines shift.
You don’t need a master plan. You need micro-proof.
Just like a kindergartener isn’t expected to do 12th grade work, you’re not meant to skip ahead.
You grow into the vision by doing what’s doable today—what already feels familiar, safe, and true. 
And when your body feels it and your brain agrees… that’s momentum without resistance.
Does it not give you a breath a fresh air to know that you don’t have to BE or DO anything on a grand scale in any given moment nor be able to know the big grand PLAN on how to get the big dreams in order to receive big dreams. The big vision clears the way to know what stays and goes with certainty so that you can then chunk down your dream to believable possible targets that then you can take familiar safe steps. So you’re only role in this is to hold the vision just as you’d enter a destination in your google maps, but all you need to do is the tiny familiar small steps of moving from one street to the next and looking for small signals on what to do along that route such as stop signs, light signals, detours, etc. that help the street you’re on be and feel more aligned, on path for the step you’re in. 
So this is what I want to anchor today:
Your nervous system doesn’t require a miracle—it just needs micro-proof.
Each small, clean, congruent action—done with FutureYou energy—signals the quantum field. It says, ‘I’m ready.’
But here’s what often happens: you take that aligned action… and your brain says, ‘That wasn’t enough to make my manifestation happen’
I want you to flip that. The tiniest moment of congruence IS the full evidence of a shift already happening. 
The energetic difference between ‘I hope this works’ and ‘I am this now’ is often invisible at first—but it becomes magnetic with repetition.
So whether you followed through perfectly on your 7-day challenge or danced with resistance, the only real question is: did I glimpse who I’m becoming? And if so, that’s everything.
 Group Check-In + Questions
 7-Day Challenge Check-In
• What did you intend to do this week?
• What actually happened—and what did that reveal about where you’re at?
• Did any resistance, procrastination, or emotional dips show up? What might they be trying to teach or protect?
 Future Casting Integration Q&A
• What part of future casting still feels fuzzy or hard to land?
• Where are you showing up from obligation vs inspiration right now?
• If you could ask your FutureYou one question right now, what would it be?
• Where do you feel “almost” ready—but not quite—and what would make it safer to shift?
 Closing Questions (optional for chat or final round)
• What one thing are you taking from today to anchor into next week?
• What version of you are you choosing to be more loyal to starting today?
