## Teaching: Why Futurecasting Can Feel Tricky

If it feels hard to hold the energy of what you want — that's not a flaw. It's your brain doing its job.

Your brain is wired for survival first, not thriving. It prioritizes scanning for threat, and looking for what's wrong and what's missing, which is why we default so easily to thoughts laced in fear, doubt, or lack. This is the negativity bias. Even when you feel joy or possibility for a moment — your survival wiring will quickly pull you back unless you consciously retrain it. Unfamiliar states are treated as risky by the nervous system, EVEN if they are desirable. Unsafe will trump desire every time unless you intervene consciously.

Your energy practices are never about getting your brain to forever stop doing its job scanning for threat, but it's about creating new pathways with awareness that give you access to something other than survival thoughts so that you shrink the time it takes to reset your nervous system and return back to your alignment where you have power of choice in the energy you respond from.

**Rewriting** helps stabilize your mind so that your body can feel safe. **Future casting** is the retraining that stabilizes your body so that your mind can feel safe so it stops offering up crap through negativity bias. We are programming a positivity bias.

We are teaching your nervous system that expansion and joy are safe, not risky or threatening.

## Mirror Neurons + The Quantum Field

Another key player in futurecasting is your mirror neurons — these are cells in your brain that automatically simulate what you observe. They help you FEEL what you OBSERVE in others. That's why you flinch when someone gets hurt, cry during movies, or feel inspired watching motivational speakers.

Mirror neurons fire automatically, unconsciously — you don't CHOOSE to activate them. This is why the energy you surround yourself with matters. It's why doomscrolling matters. Watching news matters. The company you keep matters.

The good news is that mirror neurons also allow you to borrow positive resonance from other people's wins, from what you watch and hear that uplifts and inspires, and from visualization — if you know how to do it consciously.

The quantum field works like one big cosmic mirror neuron. It "observes" your vibrational broadcast — your state of being — and begins to mirror it back through experiences and opportunities.

- Mirror neurons cause your body to experience internally what you observe externally.
- The quantum field causes your external reality to reflect and reinforce what you are pulsing internally.

Just like mirror neurons don't care what words you say — they simply respond to what you're observing — the quantum field doesn't care what you're wishing for or saying out loud. It responds to what you are consistently feeling and BEING at the vibrational level.

## The 5 Ingredients for Futurecasting to Stick

1. **Emotional salience** — it must feel personally important and meaningful and alive, not flat. This is your compelling big Why.
2. **Repetition** — practice often, so the new state feels normal. (doing the work, daily)
3. **Somatic anchoring** — feel it in your body, not just your mind. (appreciation, so muchness practices)
4. **Identity-based** — embody it as who you are becoming, not just what you hope happens. (power statements and watching it happen journal)
5. **Familiarity** — your system must begin treating this state as safe and known. (continue to take small micro steps, not quantum leaps)

We are not forcing a vision. It's about giving your system small, repeated, embodied tastes or sips of your desired state — until it becomes familiar, safe, normal… you. That's when life begins to reflect the desired goal you want as actualized in external physical form.

## Rockets of Desire (Abraham Hicks)

Abraham Hicks uses the phrase "rockets of desire" to describe the energetic signals that we launch — usually unconsciously, when you experience something you don't want (contrast), so then you automatically — vibrationally — launch a corresponding rocket of desire for what you do want. This is the process of expansion. CONTRAST, the Dips, IS the process, the cycle of expansion.

Your relationship with contrast or the dips will determine whether negativity bias kicks in, which is your nervous system afraid to take the expansion. If you intentionally view contrast as clarity and nature's way of creating expansion, you'll see it as a welcomed soul stretch. Otherwise negativity bias will override and you will stay stuck in seeing contrast as a problem.

In simple terms:
1. Contrast happens → You launch a rocket.
2. The Universe holds the vibrational reality of that desire.
3. Your work is to tune your vibration toward the desire, not against it.

## The Journaling Flow

*Grab some paper and a pen.*

### Step 1: Set Your Coordinates

Just like a rocket needs a destination, your system needs a clear target to align with.

**Prompt:** "What is the #1 goal you want to manifest?"

Choose what feels most alive for you today. Could be a tangible thing such as a dollar amount, a next step in a relationship, your physical health, a thriving business — or could be more elusive such as feeling confident, worthy, enough, proud, ease, purposeful, abundant. Choose one thing. One sentence. Don't overthink this.

Choose something you would still want even if nothing were broken or missing — something your heart genuinely longs to experience, not just fix for a current problem.

---

### Step 2: Fuel Up

Contrast gives us clarity which is fuel to refine what you are moving toward.

**Prompt:** "What experiences or situations have caused you discomfort or dissatisfaction that led you to that desire?"

The opposite of those experiences likely looks like your stated desire to manifest. Just notice how your contrast GIVES YOU clarity — this is fuel for building the right frequency.

---

### Step 3: Ignite the Engines

Your WHY is your ignition. The reason you want this gives your desire emotional power that propels it forward.

For this question, it's natural for the brain to want to answer from what you want to get rid of — but we're going to stretch beyond that.

Answer from the perspective of what you are creating, expanding into, and embodying — not what you're trying to fix or escape. Think growth, not rescue.

For example — instead of 'I want more money so I'm not stressed,' think 'I want more money so I can feel freedom, joy, and generosity.' Instead of 'I want love so I'm not lonely,' think 'I want love so I can feel cherished, radiant, and co-creative.'

**Prompt:** "Why do you want this manifestation? What feelings or experiences will it create? How does this goal align with your core values or expand your life?"

This is the emotional salience. Find your why that is anchored in expansion, not compensation. We want WHYs that speak to your expression, joy, authenticity, soul alignment — not to proving, fixing, filling a void.

---

### Step 4: Lift Off + Overcoming Inertia

This is where we begin building the felt experience brick by brick. We'll start by anchoring into one vivid, juicy moment — a detail or action that really lights you up — and then allow that energy to expand into a fuller sensory scene.

**Part 1 — Short written anchor:**

"Choose one juicy believable detail or moment of a day in the life of your FutureYou, that is evidence of the desire you want to manifest, that feels like an exciting brick to build from. It's a Watching it Happen journal moment. Big or small doesn't matter because the field is only responding to your feeling about it not the number or quantity."

Choose a location scene, an interaction, a milestone moment, something you're doing and feeling. Examples:
- "Sitting at your desk seeing a Stripe or PayPal notification for a client payment coming through — feeling calm and proud."
- "Receiving a message from a new ideal client saying 'I've been watching your posts and I'd love to work with you.'"
- "Sending out an invoice and feeling confident about your pricing — no second guessing."
- "Opening your calendar and seeing it booked with aligned client calls for the week."
- "Looking at your monthly revenue tracker and seeing a new milestone hit — feeling ease and satisfaction."

"You are not writing to get it perfect or get the full manifestation — you are writing to choose an entry point. One moment, one detail is enough to begin."

And then write one sentence or phrase to anchor it. It can be something like:
- "It's happening."
- "I'm doing this."
- "The flow is moving."
- "dialed in", "locked and loaded"
- "I'm a match to this now."

---

**Part 2 — Transition to feeling it deeper:**

Now put your pen down — from this point forward, this is about FEELING. I'm going to guide you to expand this energy of this snapshot moment and immerse in the sensory experience. You are building the feeling of your future self now — your only job is to feel it fully.

**SEE:** "Look around this future scene. What colors stand out? What is your environment like? You're looking at your phone or laptop — what's the notification, email, or message you're seeing? You glance at your work space — what do you notice about it that feels like success to you?"

**HEAR:** "What sounds are present? Are there voices? Music? What do you hear that makes this moment feel alive? What sound do you hear — the ding of a payment notification? The voice of a happy client on Zoom? Are you hearing your own inner voice saying 'Yes — this is working'?"

**SMELL:** "Is there a scent in the air? Maybe fresh flowers, a favorite perfume, or something cooking? Maybe you're sipping coffee as you read the message — how does it smell? Is there a familiar scent in your workspace — candle, flowers, essential oils?"

**TASTE:** "Are you tasting anything — a drink, a meal — that anchors you in this scene? Maybe you're tasting that first sip of coffee after a great client call — or celebrating with a favorite treat."

---

### Step 5: Breaking Through the Atmosphere

This is where you fully embody the new frequency, deeply in your body. This is where the shift happens.

**Prompts:**
- "If this is your new life — what emotions would you FEEL daily?"
- "What does this new normal feel like?"
- "What physical sensations arise in your body as you imagine this being true?"
- Does it feel like grounded confidence, relaxed shoulders, warmth, openness, tingling, expansion, lightness in your chest?
- "What emotion is rising — calm pride, ease, steady joy?"

"Let yourself linger here. The longer you can feel this, the more your nervous system learns it is safe and familiar. You are building this frequency brick by brick — this is how it becomes your new normal. If stress was gone — what are you free to feel now?"

---

### Step 6: Entering Orbit

Here we anchor this frequency into ordinary moments of life. This is how you make it a lived reality, not just a vision.

**Prompt:** "Imagine you are holding your coffee, slipping into bed, opening the door, or doing some small, familiar gesture — in this future life scene. How does it FEEL to do this ordinary thing as the version of you living this next level in your life?"

You could be:
- Drinking coffee
- Opening email
- Taking a shower
- Walking the dog
- Answering the phone
- Getting dressed

BUT — this time, you are doing it as the version of yourself who IS successful, who IS in that new frequency, in an actuality moment of your becoming. This is your new identity who's doing it!

"This is how we anchor the extraordinary into the ordinary — this is where true embodiment happens. So now you are someone who drinks their coffee proud, takes a shower relaxed and unhurried, answers your phone with unshakable confidence."

---

### Step 7: Let Go of the Boosters

**Prompts:**
- "What does this mean about who you are now?"
- "What do you know to be true about yourself that you didn't before?"
- "If your heart could speak right now about this FutureYou, what would it say?"
- "If this was always meant for you, how does it feel to finally claim it?"
- "Now that you are living this reality — what else do you see as possible?"

"Now that you've embodied this frequency, you can release old patterns that no longer serve you, and release attachment to how this will come to be. You are not responsible for the 'how.' Your job is to embody the energy — to become the signal — and to trust divine timing and the path of least resistance where you prioritize feeling good and clean energy before action. Trust the new signal, don't decode the old noise."
