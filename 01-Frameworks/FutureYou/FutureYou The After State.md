# FutureYou The After State

The After State: Teaching the Nervous System a Reference State

Segment 1 — Orientation + The After Principle
Within our imagination of our future self, what we will be imagining is our future self recalling a state that our body already knows today. 
We will be:
- Recalling a familiar physiological state (the “after” state that your body already knows
- Stabilizing that state somatically
- Allowing your mind to place that state into the future 
So today we are doing a slight mind/language twist where the future is not the source of the feeling —it’s the container for a feeling that already exists.
That’s what will help it to not feel fake.
You’re not trying to feel something new.
You’re letting the future “borrow” a state your body already trusts. 
So first we are not going to imagine our future. We are going to recall a state in our body and then imagine ourselves re-calling or borrowing that state 
What we’re working with is something much more fundamental.Certain states in the nervous system are structural, not situational.
Relief.
Certainty.
The feeling when a decision lands.
The body after tension resolves.
These aren’t tied to one specific event — they’re patterns your nervous system already knows how to run.
So….and here’s what’s important…even if you’ve never had this exact outcome you are wanting to manifest that you’re working toward…your body already knows what it feels like when something is handled. Received. Certain. 
We are not trying to create a new feeling.
We’re teaching your system a reference state — a familiar internal pattern — and our brains, love patterns and efficiency in accessing them. 
We do this so that the future doesn’t feel unknown… it feels recognizable. And thereby a safe pattern that your brain accepts without mind thought sabotage. 

Segment 2 — Finding the Structural ‘After’ State (10 minutes)
I want you to bring to mind a moment in your life where something resolved inside you.
Not necessarily something big.
Not necessarily related to what you want right now.
Just a moment where:
- uncertainty ended
- a decision landed
- or something you’d been holding finally settled.
This is not the adrenaline moment. Not a big rush or celebration. 
Let your body move forward in time —hours or days later after the hype of that moment. Maybe that’s even today. Just far enough that life had continued.Far enough that your nervous system stood down. We are looking for the moment after the thing happened, where what remained was steadiness. Integration as this is me now, this is my life now. This is normal and happening. Pause 20–30 seconds. You’re not remembering the situation. You’re remembering the state. Then deepen the somatic awareness:
Notice what your body does when it recognizes: This is just how things are now.

Segment 3 — Installing the Reference State (5 minutes)
Now we’re going to stabilize this state so your system can recognize it again later.
Place your feet on the ground.Feel the weight of your body drop into the chair or the floor — like gravity is allowed to do its job
Let your spine lengthen slightly — supported, not rigid.
Gently let tour shoulders roll back and down — not to fix anything — just to signal completion. 
Unclench your jaw and leave a little space between your teeth.
Let your tongue rest on the floor of your mouth.
This directly cues safety.
Let your hands rest open.
Let your eyes soften — as if you’re looking at something familiar, not scanning for danger.
This alone drops vigilance.
Inhale gently through your nose…
and exhale longer than you inhale.
Again… slow inhale… longer exhale
When your body shifts, the signal changes. You’re teaching your nervous system:
this is the posture of ‘handled.’

Now let a neutral sentence arise — something simple and true.
Not an affirmation.
Not hype. 
- This is handled.
- I don’t need to push.
- This is just how things are now.
- It worked out.
- I’m on the other side of this now.
- This is just how things are now. 
Let this be the sentence you’d think later — when you’re no longer replaying it.
The sentence that arrives once something has already integrated.
Say it once silently.
Let your body respond.
Say it again…
and notice the settling.
This becomes your reference state —
not tied to an outcome,
but to a pattern your body recognizes as safe and complete.

Segment 4 — Borrowing the State and Letting the Future Wear It (5 minutes)
Now I want you to stay exactly where you are in your body.
Don’t change your breath.
Don’t change your posture.
Don’t reach for a new feeling.
The steadiness you’re in right now —
this is the state.
We’re not upgrading it.
We’re not intensifying it.
We’re letting the future borrow it.

Wearing the State
Imagine this state like something you’re wearing —not a costume, not something you put on for effect —but something that fits so naturally you don’t think about it.
Feel it around your shoulders.
In your chest. In the way your body holds itself when nothing is being asked of you. This is the version of you that your futureyou will meet.

Now, without moving your body at all, let your awareness step slightly forward in time.
You don’t leave this body.You bring this body with you.
Notice that you’re still breathing the same way. Still grounded. Still steady.
See yourself moving through an ordinary day in the future —not fixing, not striving, not checking.
Just being you…with this same steadiness in your body.
This is how the nervous system learns familiarity instead of fear.
