# FutureYou EASE

## The E.A.S.E. Model

Your "Future You" has a specific emotional frequency, way of thinking, and way of moving through life. The more you can live in that frequency now, the faster the outer world shifts to match it.

The challenge? Life knocks us out of that state — negative emotions, stress, doubt, or overwhelm can pull us back into old patterns.

That's where E.A.S.E. comes in. E.A.S.E. is a 4-step reset process you can use anytime you feel off, so you can realign with your Future You quickly and keep moving toward what you want.

---

## What Speeds Manifestation More Than Perfect Alignment

Some people believe if they could just stay in alignment every minute of the day, they'd get their manifestations faster. It sounds logical, but here's what actually happens — the harder you try to hold perfect alignment, the more tense and self-critical you become, and that tension quietly broadcasts "It's not here yet."

**Why "all-day alignment" doesn't automatically speed things up:**
- It's not sustainable for most people. Trying to hold perfect alignment 24/7 often creates tension, hyper-vigilance, and pressure — which ironically lowers your frequency.
- The field responds to consistency, not perfection. Abraham Hicks, Neville Goddard, and even Joe Dispenza all emphasize that what matters most is returning to your desired state regularly, not never leaving it.

**Balance Bike Metaphor:** Alignment is like riding a balance bike. When you're learning, you wobble, lean too far one way, then the other. You don't keep the bike perfectly upright all the time — you just keep making little adjustments to stay generally on course. If you tried to lock yourself perfectly still so you never tipped even a tiny bit, you'd actually fall faster. Manifestation works the same way.

**"It's not about never wobbling — it's about knowing how to rebalance quickly."**

**What speeds it up more effectively:**
- Frequent recalibration: Every time you drift, notice and shift back without judgment. This builds emotional muscle and keeps you mostly in range.
- State + action pairing: Using your aligned state to take real-world steps creates landing places for your manifestation, which accelerates results.
- Letting go between alignments: Alignment works best when you can "set it and forget it" for chunks of time, rather than hovering over it mentally all day.

It's not about never leaving alignment — it's about how quickly and easily you come back.

## E.A.S.E. Formula

**E — Evoke Awareness**
- Shift from autopilot into conscious presence.
- Simply notice what's happening in your mind, body, and emotions.
- This works whether you're calm or triggered — it's about becoming aware enough to choose your energy instead of letting default patterns run the show.

**A — Anchor Your Identity**
- Connect to the feelings and qualities of your Future You without focusing on a specific goal yet.
- Use past memories, imagined scenarios, or sensory triggers to bring those qualities into your body.

**S — See the Scene**
- With your Future You state activated, picture a short, vivid "proof" moment of your manifestation being real.
- Keep it simple and sensory-rich.

**E — Engage the Field**
- Take one small, aligned action immediately from that state.
- This gives your energy a landing strip in the physical world.

## Detailed Facilitation Script

### Step 1 — Evoke Awareness (2 min)

"We start by evoking awareness. Just pause for a moment and check in — what's present in your mind and body right now? Where is your energy right now? What's happening in your body?

Just take a moment to notice — are your shoulders tense or relaxed? Is your breath shallow or deep? Notice any thoughts or emotions without trying to change them yet. Just become aware. You are the Watcher, the observer of your thoughts."

**Prompts:**
- What one word would you use to describe your current state?
- Now ask yourself: "Is this the energy I want to carry forward, or do I want to shift, pivot or STEP INTO my Future You state?"

The essence of this first step is to shift from autopilot/reactivity into conscious presence.

**How "Evoke Awareness" looks in practice:**
- **Notice:** "What's happening in me right now? Where's my energy?"
- **Name:** Call out what you're feeling, without judgment — e.g., "I'm feeling rushed" or "I'm feeling doubtful."
- **Pause:** Create a conscious gap between being the feeling and observing the feeling in your body/mind — and in that space you create the superpower of choice in your next move.
- **Choose:** Decide that you're going to shift into your Future You energy before continuing.

---

### Step 2 — Anchor Your Identity (2 min)

"This is about generating the state — abundance, love, safety, joy — without needing to attach it to a specific manifestation right away. If you go straight to imagining the thing you want before you've shifted your emotional baseline, your mind will mix in doubt or resistance. Think of it as charging your emotional battery first.

Think about your Future You — the version of you who already lives the life you want. What are one or two emotions or qualities you feel every day? Confident, peaceful, abundant, playful, loved, a pleasure to be around.

Now, think of any time in your past when you've felt those emotions — even in a completely different context. A sensory sip. Let that memory come alive. Notice where you were, what you saw, what you heard. Let yourself fully feel that version of you right now."

**Prompts:**
- What qualities feel strongest for your Future You?
- What past moment helps you feel those emotions now?

*Note: Feeling without agenda is simply about getting into the emotional frequency you want to live in — you could stop here and still draw in great things, because you're in a receptive state. But if there's something you specifically want, aiming the feeling toward it helps collapse the timeline faster because you're broadcasting both the emotion and the coordinates. That brings us to step 3.*

---

### Step 3 — See the Scene (2 min)

With your Future You state alive in your body, imagine a short, specific moment that proves your manifestation is real. Find an evidence-based moment in your FutureYou's life.

Keep it under 20 seconds long — just one loop of a moment (boomerangs). Not too long and detailed that you start to doubt it as inauthentic.

Maybe it's a text from a client, an email saying 'Payment received,' or a loved one congratulating you. Keep it simple but sensory rich. You're going for potency, not bigness.

See it through your own eyes. Hear the sounds, feel the sensations, let the emotion fill you. Neville called this "first-person experience" — it tricks the subconscious into coding it as memory.

**Build the Scene in Layers:**
- Start with one anchor detail: the texture of the steering wheel in your new car, the warmth of a hand during a congratulations handshake.
- Add in one sensory layer at a time: sound, smell, temperature, lighting.
- Each time you add a detail, let the emotion stay alive — the emotion is the glue.

**Anchor It in the Body:**
- Touch your heart or place a hand on your chest.
- Smile, nod, or take a deep breath of satisfaction in the scene.
- These micro-body actions tell your brain, "This is actually happening."

**Prompts:**
- "What's the moment you're seeing?"
- "What details make it feel the most real?"

Repetition in the same emotional state turns it into a known memory for your subconscious, which starts organizing reality to match.

---

### Step 4 — Engage the Field (1–2 min)

From this Future You energy, ask yourself: What's one small, obvious action I can take in the next hour to move toward this?

Example: Sending a follow-up email to a potential client or posting your offer online.

Trust the first idea that comes to mind — it doesn't have to be big. Write it down, and commit to doing it today. This is how you give your energy a place to land.

**Prompts:**
- "What's the smallest next step that feels doable from here?"
- "When will you do it?"

## Closing

"When you start with the feeling, add the direction, and then take a step, you're working both the seen and unseen sides of creation.

E.A.S.E. is something you can use as often as you need. The point isn't to stay in alignment perfectly all day — it's to return to it quickly and take a step from there. That's how you build the energy of your Future You until it becomes your normal."

---

## Quick Daily Version

Do this every day, multiple times per day. Spend 1 minute on each of the 4 steps.

1. Simply notice what's happening in your mind, body, and emotions. What one word would you use to describe your current state? Name it. Ask yourself: Is this the energy I want to carry forward?

2. Connect to the FEELINGS and qualities of your Future You. Once you name those, use PAST memories to bring those emotions/qualities into your body. Vividly recall a past memory on ANY OTHER subject than your current desire, where you felt that feeling, and re-feel it all over again.

3. With your Future You emotional state primed and activated, picture a short, vivid "proof" moment of your actual desired manifestation being real. What's that couple of seconds in that moment you can replay over and over, feeling it with senses? BE in that moment. What do you hear, see, touch, smell? Give it color and texture as you replay it.

4. Take one small, aligned action immediately TODAY from that energized, inspired state. What's the first thing that comes to mind that feels exciting, interesting, or like an ah-ha thought? Don't overthink it. This gives your energy a landing strip in the physical world. If nothing occurred to you, repeat the previous 3 steps.

## Core Truth

You can't control anything other than your own vibrational state. And your vibrational state controls EVERYTHING.

Manifestation is about consciously building and living in the energy of your Future You. That starts with awareness. If you don't know what state you're in, you can't choose a better one. And if you can't choose a better one, you will continue attracting circumstances aligned with the state you are in.
