## Power Poses (3 min total — 30 sec each)

Teach the 3 power poses. Let participants know we'll hold each one for 30 seconds while embodying the healthiest version of themselves, based on the details they just journaled.

This is the real work. Futurecasting is the energetic preparation that makes the physical transformation sustainable.

### 1. Victory Pose

**Movement:**
- Stand tall with feet hip-width apart.
- Raise your arms overhead in a wide "V" shape, fingers spread.
- Lift your chin slightly, open your chest, and hold the posture for 30–60 seconds.
- Optional: Add a deep inhale and exhale through the nose as you hold it.

**Why:** This posture mimics the universal body language of success and triggers a physiological confidence response. Harvard research shows it can increase testosterone and reduce cortisol. It tells your body, "I've already won." It trains your nervous system to expect wellness.

**Cue:** "Stand like the version of you who already crossed the finish line. Feel the power in your breath, your spine, your reach. You are claiming your wholeness. This is how a victorious, thriving body stands. You've outgrown depletion. You are expanding into strength, vitality, and radiant health with every breath."

### 2. Superwoman / Wonder Woman Pose

**Movement:**
- Stand with feet shoulder-width apart.
- Place both hands firmly on your hips.
- Pull your shoulders back, chest lifted, chin level.
- Hold for 30 seconds while breathing slowly and deeply.

**Why:** Known as a "power pose," this stance programs the nervous system to feel secure and in command. It promotes feelings of personal power, presence, and grounded authority. It also signals the body that you are safe to thrive — physically, mentally, and emotionally.

**Cue:** "Stand like someone who knows they belong here — in this body, on this earth, in their power. Own your space. Own your energy. This is the posture of someone who trusts their body to heal, rise, and regenerate. Let every breath tell your cells: we are safe, we are strong, we are unstoppable."

### 3. Shake Off the Old

**Movement:**
- Stand with soft knees. Begin to gently shake your hands.
- Let it ripple into your arms, shoulders, hips, and legs.
- Bounce if it feels natural. Let your jaw loosen and breathe.

**Why:** Shaking resets the nervous system, discharges stress, and releases stagnant energy. It's a primal way of saying, "I'm done holding this."

**Cue:** "Let your body release what it no longer needs. Shake out the stress. Shake out the tension that isn't yours. You are making space for something new. This is how your body clears old frequencies so it can remember vitality. Every shake sends the message: I choose health. I choose healing. I choose energy."

### 4. Brush It Off (Energetic Clearing)

**Movement:**
- Use your hands to physically brush downward from your head to your toes.
- Sweep down your arms, chest, back, belly, and legs.
- Then scoop upward from the ground like you're pulling fresh energy into your body.

**Why:** Brushing clears energetic residue and signals your body to reset and refresh. Scooping in upward motion tells the body: "Now we rise."

**Cue:** "Brush off the residue of yesterday. Brush off what clings but no longer serves. Now gather what you're ready to feel — pull in clarity, strength, and vitality. Brush your body like it matters — because it does. Let it know you're listening, you're leading, and you believe in its ability to heal."

### 5. Rise + Root Pulse (Ground + Recharge)

**Movement:**
- Inhale and rise up onto your toes, arms reaching overhead.
- Exhale and drop gently onto your heels, arms falling to your sides.
- Repeat for 5–6 full breaths.

**Why:** This pulse activates the legs and spine while syncing breath with movement. Rising and rooting at once creates both expansion and steadiness.

**Cue:** "Rise into your next evolution. Reach for the frequency of your future self. Then root it — lock it in. You are stable, grounded, and rising all at once. This is how a thriving body breathes. Let the rise signal your energy expanding. Let the root remind your body: I am safe to be strong. I am grounded in health."

### 6. Command the Cells (Claim Your Power)

**Movement:**
- Stand tall with one hand on your heart and one on your lower belly.
- Soften your gaze or close your eyes.
- Speak or think the affirmations below, slowly and with conviction.

**Affirmations:**
- "I am the leader of this body."
- "Every cell listens to my thoughts."
- "We are healing, growing, and thriving."
- "My body responds to love and direction."

**Why:** Your cells respond to the emotional and energetic signals you send. Speaking with authority creates coherence between mind, body, and intention.

**Cue:** "Let your voice guide your biology. Speak to your body with reverence and clarity. You are not waiting for your body to become whole. You are leading it there. Every affirmation is a blueprint. Your body hears it. Your cells believe it. You are the author of your healing, the conductor of your energy, and the embodiment of radiant health."

### Final Anchor: Seal the Shift

**Movement:**
- Place both hands over your heart.
- Take 3 slow, deep breaths in and out.
- Feel the sensations in your body.

**Why:** Breath locks in the nervous system's new set point. Your touch anchors the shift physically and emotionally.

**Cue:** "This is your new baseline. Your body knows the way. You are the embodiment of vitality."
