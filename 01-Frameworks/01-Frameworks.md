## The Big Picture

Tanya's entire body of work orbits one central idea:

> **You are not broken. You are misaligned. Come home to yourself, step into who you're becoming, and the life follows.**

Every framework either helps someone **understand where they are** (awareness), **release what's blocking them** (clearing), or **step into who they're becoming** (becoming).

```
AWARENESS → CLEARING → BECOMING
```

> For the most human, emotional expression of why all of this matters, read [[03-Resources/moms_eulogy]]. It is the philosophical north star behind every framework in this vault.

```
Thought & Emotion Work
        ↓
   (creates awareness)
        ↓
Alignment & Frequency ←→ Belief & Blocks
        ↓                      ↓
   FutureYou Identity Work ←←←←
        ↓
  FYF Exercises (in-session tools)
```

The typical client journey moves **top to bottom** — first understanding their inner world, then clearing blocks, then stepping into becoming, then anchoring it through practice.
