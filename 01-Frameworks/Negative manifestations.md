# Negative Manifestations

## Contrast with Relationships

Let's get clarity on why it has gone the way it has gone.

---

### Step 1

Write a couple thoughts down about finding your partner.

- What do you THINK about finding your partner? What's your unfiltered response?
- What do you FEEL about finding a partner?

---

### Step 2

Write down: "This energy is in my body."

This energy is not brand new. This energy has been there for a while. That's why things have been how they've been.

You have a lot of data to support why this is true — data that 99% of people would agree with you on. But if that's your energy, you cannot attract outside of this.

Draw a square around your words and at the top write down "JAIL CELL."

As long as that's your default answer, that is going to be your reality.

**How was I a match to that?**

Looking at something long enough is enough to become a match to it. Whatever you look at, you see more of.

This energy is here because of one of these reasons:
- You can partly blame your parents; parents' energy permeates you as a child. So the momentum could have been given to you and grew momentum. You didn't do something to attract it.
- You were with somebody doing something shitty and kept looking at it and telling them to change, reminding them that they don't do this and they should do that — and just by continuing to look at it, it grew in you.
- The shitty thing happening is called contrast but here with purpose FOR you. Contrast is helping you to clarify and define what you want. Contrast is the distance between where you are now and where you want to be. And the more contrast, the greater distance exists, the greater the pain, and the greater the incentive to shift your energy and manifest the contrast. People who don't have a lot of problems don't want strongly. People who have more problems want more strongly. So you can't really define contrast as a bad manifestation.
- It's a lesson that propels you by bringing attention to the problem that's blocking you. Isn't it nice to get a magnifying glass on something that's prevalent that's not helpful and have awareness of it?
- The dip gives kinetic energy to make it up the next hill for you to go farther faster. It's a booster rocket.

In any case, the past doesn't matter. It's zero relevance today. The only thing relevant today is the story and energy living in your body.

---

### Step 3

See if you can wiggle into the sentiment of "I know exactly what is happening. It's not a mystery."

Move from "I don't know" into "I do actually know" — recognizing it was one of the 3 reasons above:
1. You were carrying an energy given to you as a child
2. You got into a habit of looking at things you didn't want more than what you do want
3. You are going through a human experience to experience something that you don't want so that you can clearly define and move into what you do want

The past doesn't matter. I actually do know why. However it's nice to know it doesn't matter. There are many reasons why whatever may have happened, happened. But literally now it's irrelevant. What matters is how I answer the question: what do I THINK about finding my partner and how do I FEEL about it? How I answer that question is going to be my biggest lever.

---

### Step 4: Strategy to Get Into the New Frequency

**Rules:**
- Can't live in the jail cell of that old story and find a juicy and new one
- But we also can't say anything so positive about what's coming that our brain won't believe it
- It's a new way to answer these questions that feels more nourishing in my body and genuinely feels good in your body and like a breath of fresh air — or creates space or lifts the heaviness just a bit. Able to give yourself credit, breathe deep and let yourself off the hook or someone else off the hook.

**Litmus check on the rewrite:**
- Do I believe what I just wrote?
- Does it feel better than what I said before?

However you answered that question is a wide neuropathway in your brain — easy to go down that path. When we rewrite, this is now a baby neuropathway that will need watering every day. When you do the rewrite for the first time, it's like putting a seed in the ground and it needs to be watered and tended to every day to grow and widen that pathway and increase the energy so that your default energy shifts.

Notice that when you're re-running something, your mind is in the past and a little bit in the present, whereas when we start brewing something up we are more future oriented.

---

### Brewing Up the New Energy

What would you be like with your perfect partner or whatever the manifestation is that you're looking for?

- What would I want to be like?
- What would I like to be like?
- How would I like to feel?
- How would I like to feel about myself? About them? About the situation?
- What would it feel like?
- What would it feel like to watch TV?
- What would it feel like to do ordinary things? And the big things?
- What would it feel like getting coffee together?
- What would it feel like eating lunch?
