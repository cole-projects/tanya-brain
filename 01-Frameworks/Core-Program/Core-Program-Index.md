# Core Program — 7-Week Curriculum

> The conceptual backbone of Tanya's coaching methodology. These seven weeks form a complete arc from awareness through belief formation — each building on the last. Everything else in the brain is the application of what lives here.

---

## The Sequence

| Week | Topic | Core Insight |
|---|---|---|
| [[Week-1-Circumstance-vs-Thought\|Week 1]] | Circumstance vs. Thought | Your power begins when you separate what happened from what you made it mean |
| [[Week-2-Catch-Call-Choose\|Week 2]] | Catch It, Call It Out, Choose Again | You are not your thoughts — you are the one who notices them |
| [[Week-3-Bypassing-vs-Rewriting\|Week 3]] | The Energy Behind the Words | Bypassing escapes discomfort. Rewriting expands your capacity to hold it. |
| [[Week-4-Emotional-Holding\|Week 4]] | Emotional Holding / Self-Connection | Alignment has an order of operations. Hold before you shift. |
| [[Week-5-Regulation-Stabilization\|Week 5]] | Regulation / Stabilization | When holding alone isn't enough, the body needs active support to settle. |
| [[Week-6-Integration\|Week 6]] | Integration + Bridge to Reorientation | The full sequence: Connect → Stabilize → Shift. You can't force alignment. |
| [[Week-7-Belief-Formation\|Week 7]] | Belief Formation | Familiar doesn't mean true. Beliefs feel like facts until you see them as patterns. |
