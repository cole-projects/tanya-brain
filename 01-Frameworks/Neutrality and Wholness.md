# Neutrality and Wholeness

## The State Beyond Lack and Abundance

His answer to what it feels like to NOT feel lack was deep.

Best line: "It's not even on my radar to be something I'm supposed to be concerned about. It's just neutral."

He is saying he no longer lives from the brain needing to make meaning and label things as lack vs abundance. It all just is. He's not concerned about abundance any more than he is not concerned about lack. It's just not on the radar to be differentiating and being on the lookout.

This is the state of true neutrality and wholeness — not living from either pole, but existing as the awareness that observes both without getting caught in either.
