# Yin Yang

## The Energy Lens

Traditionally, Yin (black) is the receptive, intuitive, inward, being-state. Yang (white) is the active, expressive, outward, human-doing state.

This interpretation — black = Source/being, white = human/action — fits beautifully with how the MESH framework teaches energy. It's an applied metaphor, not the original doctrine.

**The essence is the same:**
- Traditional language: Yin = receptive, intuitive, inward, being. Yang = active, outward, doing.
- MESH language: Yin = Source energy, being. Yang = human action, doing.

---

## The Core Teaching

You cannot get to where you want to be from an action-only strategy. You've got to enlist the knowledge of your inner being, the energy of source. You've got to get up to vibrational speed with who you really are.

Yin and Yang together form the complete bridge: being grounded in Source (Yin) while expressing through aligned action (Yang). Neither is complete without the other.
