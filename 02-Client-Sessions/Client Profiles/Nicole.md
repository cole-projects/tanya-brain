# Client Profile — Nicole

> This file is the memory layer for Nicole. Update after every session.
> Claude reads this before responding to ensure continuity across conversations.

---

## Core Wound
- **Primary:** Not Worthy
- **Secondary:** Not Supported
- **Belief underneath:** "I have to earn my worth by carrying others' problems. If I'm not producing or fixing, I don't deserve to rest or receive."

---

## What They Came In With
- Ups and downs in energy — 50/50 fluctuation
- Building her business with obstacles around finding a location
- Struggling to stay consistent with energy work
- Feeling overwhelmed, too much on her plate

---

## The Deeper Issue
- Internalized belief that she must fix and carry others' problems to matter
- Treats energy work like a to-do list, which drains the joy out of it
- Learned early that her own needs weren't a priority — parents' problems were dumped on her
- Looks outside herself for confirmation (borrowing other people's formulas, vibes, approaches)

---

## Recurring Patterns
- Gets into "to-do list mode" even with energy work — turns joy into obligation
- Catches herself outsourcing her knowing (Tim's formula, a chiropractor's approach)
- Swings between amazing days and "reality just hit" crashes
- Creates business obstacles, then resolves them through creative pivots
- Tries to apply someone else's formula instead of trusting her own inner knowing
