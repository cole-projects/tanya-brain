# Client Profile — Kerry

> This file is the memory layer for Kerry. Update after every session.
> Claude reads this before responding to ensure continuity across conversations.

---

## Core Wound
- **Primary:** Not Supported
- **Secondary:** Not Safe
- **Belief underneath:** "I have to control everything and everyone to feel safe. If I'm not managing, things fall apart. I need permission to do things for myself."

---

## What They Came In With
- Family stress (kids, husband, house responsibilities)
- Wants passive income to have autonomy and financial safety
- Strong "block against money" — can't even land on a number that feels good
- Feeling less patient and more controlling with kids and husband
- Underlying goal: financial independence = freedom from needing anyone's permission

---

## The Deeper Issue
- Control as a safety mechanism learned early — "busy = safe"
- Scarcity mindset around money masking a deeper fear about independence and autonomy
- Takes on husband's anxiety and stress as hers to fix
- Doesn't allow herself ease or rest because rest feels like losing control
- The real wound: needs permission to do things for herself (money = the permission slip)

---

## Recurring Patterns
- Starts days energized from energy work, then the day "molds itself" and her plan dissolves
- Oscillates between controlling outcomes and surrendering to them
- Takes on family members' emotions as her responsibility to manage
- Compares her passive income progress to others'
- But real progress is happening: less controlling, more supportive with kids, enjoying husband's company more

## Key Breakthroughs
- Disney trip: Reframed sickness and husband's anxiety → ended with annual pass and more family time
- Realized: Allowing > controlling consistently leads to better outcomes
- Got that she feels *"best in her body since having kids"* — major somatic shift
- Understood: The money goal is really about freedom to make choices without asking permission (even a haircut)
- Saw: Ease in relationships is *proof* the mechanics work — same mechanics will work for money
- Recognized: Real desire is not $1M but freedom to travel and pay off the house
- Accepted: The byproducts (better relationships, more ease, more joy) are the actual gift

---

## Still Stuck On
- Fully trusting that passive income is on its way (feels like it's "underground, not sprouting yet")
- Naming a specific money goal — has a block against picking a number
- Releasing the belief that she needs permission for self-care
- Allowing her goals to evolve as she becomes a new version of herself
- Complete ease with not controlling how things unfold

---

## Their Language
- *"It just feels different"*
- *"I feel calmer about it"*
- *"I'm more trying to be there as support"* (vs. fix it)
- *"Why did I feel like I needed permission?"*
- *"The roots are taking root"* (seed underground metaphor resonates deeply)
- *"The juice of it is everything in the middle"*
- *"It's coming to us"*

---

## What They Respond Well To
- Celebrating shifts already made in other areas (proof the mechanics work)
- Teaching her to notice her own knowing and wisdom
- Pause practice creating space before she reacts
- Gentle reframes: control → allowing, panic → calm presence
- Stories and metaphors that land (Disney, radiator, generous assumptions)
- Validating that wanting ease is valid AND there's another way to get there
- Teaching her she's becoming a new identity that can hold the goal she wants
- Permission to enjoy the journey — not just chase the endpoint

**Mid-to-Deep** (~5 months). Solid progress in relationships and overall ease. The money piece remains stuck because it represents a deeper autonomy and permission wound. Next work: address the real desire underneath the money goal (freedom, not a number) and trust the same mechanics she's already proven work in other areas.
