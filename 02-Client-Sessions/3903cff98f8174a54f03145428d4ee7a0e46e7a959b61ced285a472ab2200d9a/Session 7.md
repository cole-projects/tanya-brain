Someone who knows you well enough to skip the consolation and go straight to the exact thing that cracks you open. That takes a level of knowing most people never build with each other.

**Client:** I think I used to always be that one and then somewhere along the way I stopped because maybe I looked like the crazy friend, that you’re responsible on, the messy one and I didn’t like that idea of people seeing me that way, so I became more careful, less highs and less lows.
