# Coaching Question Bank

Tanya's source bank of coaching questions. These are the raw questions the six questioning moves in [[00-MOC/CONVERSATION-GUIDELINES]] were extracted from. Use this as a reference — not a script. The principles that govern when and how to use questions live in the Questioning Moves section of CONVERSATION-GUIDELINES.

See also: [[01-Frameworks/Coaching-Session-Outline]]

---

Based on our conversation and the options you are considering, I heard some self commitments. Can you recap?
What part of this do you want to go into deeper?
How does this pattern continue to serve you?
What is the thought that's been destroying your confidence?
What is a constructive thought that fuels your confidence?
How do you envision yourself when you get there?
What would you like to feel instead?
How would your most wise and loving self handle this?
What is beyond that?
Who do you need to be in order to be successful, heard, at comfort with yourself and balanced as you begin whatever you want to do?
What's your current thinking around this?
What's the good you see in this?
What's the good showing up in this?
If you were assured that there was good in this bad, what might it be?
What do you want to believe, on purpose?
What values that you hold does your decision meet?
If you were to pay attention to your needs, what would shift in how you respond and interact with yourself or others?
If you are on the other side of this argument, how would you see things going better?
What core values do you want to be bringing into this situation for the situation to be more successful?
What do you want to reflect on further?
What responsibilities in this do you own?
What does this reinforce for you that you want to incorporate into your way of being?
Is there anything else you feel you need to expose to better understand your situation?
What have you done in the past that has been successful in similar situations?
What strength do you have that could help you overcome this in order to accomplish what you'd like to accomplish?
How would you like to approach this part of your life?
What's an inquiry that would support you to know what you want to do when you are feeling this way?
How do you want to show up so that you can apply your wholeness to what you are doing?
What about the situation is great?
What is one element of this that might need improvement?
Where do you have choice to change something about your situation?
What are you experiencing in yourself now that you didn't have access to before?
What awareness do you have as we're moving along here?
What do you know now that you were not aware of previously?
What if anything has been revealed to you that brought you deeper awareness about yourself that could be of use?
How do you want to be or to start being with this?
What bird's-eye view of this have you been living in and what view would best support you now going forward?
What's showing up for you that reflects your way of being, what you value, how you view your world and what you are still choosing?
What if anything is meaningful in that for you?
What was the value you received in return for your sacrifice of the intention you set out with, and was it worth the price in the end to choose it again knowing this price?
How did your choice feed and fuel your soul, or not?
What do you already know to be true?
What core strengths would you like to bring forward to help you accomplish and ensure your success moving forward?
What did you learn from this session that you can now apply to your life or the situation?
What do you likely to learn from the action steps you are going to put in place?
How might this be beneficial to you in other parts of your life?
What learnings are coming out of this for you about your situation or about yourself?
What do you need to put in place to support your journey?
What do you need to be complete around this topic?
What was a new or reinforced insight or learning for you?
What is a new awareness coming out of the session and how do you want to apply that?
Is there anything you need to do or share to have closure on this topic?
What's an inquiry that would support you to know what you want to do when you get some clarity around it?
What further reflection or question do you want to continue to ponder or reflect upon between now and our next session?
What additional support can help you stay in remembrance of your actions to take that will give you the energy to follow through on these important commitments to yourself?
Do I hear a commitment you'd like to make or is this just a good idea at this stage?
What will you do differently as a result of this learning?
